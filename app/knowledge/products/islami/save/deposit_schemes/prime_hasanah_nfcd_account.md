# Prime Hasanah NFCD Account

**Tagline:** Global Earnings, Islamic Banking

Prime Hasanah Non-Resident Foreign Currency Deposit (NFCD) Account enables foreign currency savings and investment for Bangladeshi expatriates, foreign nationals, and international workers with 1-12 month flexible tenors and multi-currency support.

---

## Overview

Prime Hasanah NFCD Account serves the unique financial needs of globally-mobile customers—expatriate Bangladeshis, foreign nationals working abroad or in Bangladesh, and international workers. With multi-currency support (USD, GBP, EUR, JPY) and flexible 1-12 month tenors, it provides a secure platform to manage foreign earnings while earning competitive returns.

## FAQs

**Q: Can Bangladeshi citizens abroad open NFCD?**
A: Yes! Bangladeshi expatriates working abroad are primary target customers.

**Q: What if I have multiple nationalities?**
A: If one is Bangladeshi + working abroad, qualifies as expatriate. Otherwise, foreign national category.

**Q: Can I deposit in multiple currencies?**
A: Yes. Open separate accounts for each currency (USD, GBP, EUR, JPY).

**Q: What if I need funds before tenor ends?**
A: Early encashment possible but at Savings Account rate (lower returns).

**Q: Is profit taxable?**
A: Yes. Per applicable tax laws in operating jurisdiction (typically ~10-20%).

**Q: Can I visit Bangladesh and withdraw?**
A: Yes. Use debit card at Prime Bank branches or withdraw at Dhaka main office.

**Q: What happens if I return to Bangladesh?**
A: Can convert to regular Savings Account or maintain NFCD if still abroad-connected.

**Q: How are wire transfers processed?**
A: Via SWIFT internationally, within 2-3 business days typically.

**Q: Can companies open joint accounts?**
A: Yes. Multiple authorized signatories possible with board resolution.

**Q: Is deposit insured?**
A: Yes. Covered under deposit insurance up to limit (foreign currency conversion applied).
