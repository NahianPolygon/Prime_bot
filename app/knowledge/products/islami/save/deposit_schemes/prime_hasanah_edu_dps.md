# Prime Hasanah Edu DPS

**Tagline:** Education Investment, Future Security

Prime Hasanah Edu DPS is an education-focused monthly installment scheme following Islamic Mudaraba principles, offering 9% profit across 5/10/15/20 year tenors designed to build education funds for children.

---

## Overview

Prime Hasanah Edu DPS is a specialized recurring deposit scheme dedicated to education savings. With flexible long tenors (5-20 years) matching education timelines and fixed 9% profit rate, it empowers parents and guardians to systematically build substantial education funds for their children's future.

---

## What is Edu DPS?

**Prime Hasanah Edu DPS** provides:
- **Education-Focused Savings** for child's future
- **Long Tenors:** 5, 10, 15, or 20 years
- **Monthly Installments:** BDT 500 to 120,000
- **Fixed 9% Profit:** All tenors
- **Islamic Mudaraba:** Shariah-compliant

Perfect for parents and guardians wanting to secure their child's education through disciplined, systematic savings.

## Eligibility

## How to Apply?

### Required Documents

1. **Completed Personal Savings Scheme Account Opening Form**
   - All details filled
   - Signature required
   - Dated

2. **Active Linked CASA Account**
   - Current or Savings account
   - With Prime Bank
   - For automatic deduction

3. **Identity Documents**
   - NID, Passport, or Birth Certificate
   - Parent/guardian and child copies

4. **Child's Documents** (if minor beneficiary)
   - Birth certificate
   - School ID or certificate
   - Parent/guardian mandate

### Opening Process

1. Visit Prime Bank branch
2. Request "Prime Hasanah Edu DPS" form
3. Choose tenor (5/10/15/20 years)
4. Specify child's education goal
5. Decide monthly installment
6. Provide linked account details
7. Complete scheme form
8. Submit all documents
9. Receive scheme confirmation
10. Monthly deduction starts

## FAQs

**Q: When does maturity coincide with college age?**
A: Choose tenor so maturity year matches college entry year (typically 15-20 years from birth).

**Q: Can I withdraw before maturity?**
A: Yes, but profit significantly reduced. Complete tenure recommended.

**Q: What if I miss a payment?**
A: Scheme suspended. Resume to continue. Maintain consistency for goal.

**Q: Can multiple schemes be opened?**
A: Yes. Open separate schemes for each child or multiple education goals.

**Q: Is profit guaranteed?**
A: Yes. 9% locked for your tenure.

**Q: Can minors be beneficiaries?**
A: Yes. Parent/guardian opens and operates on their behalf.

**Q: Is profit subject to tax?**
A: Yes. Income tax per applicable laws (~10% typical).

**Q: Can I borrow against this?**
A: Yes. Up to 90% of deposits available for educational emergencies.
