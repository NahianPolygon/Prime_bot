# Prime Hasanah Term Deposit

**Tagline:** Saving Today, For A Comfortable Tomorrow

Prime Hasanah Term Deposit is a flexible fixed deposit scheme with tenure options from 1-36 months, following Islamic Mudaraba principles, offering attractive profit rates and auto-renewal options.

---

## Overview

Prime Hasanah Term Deposit provides a flexible, Islamic-compliant fixed deposit investment for any timeline between 1-36 months. With a minimum deposit of BDT 10,000 and attractive profit rates, it combines short-term liquidity with steady returns, perfect for those saving toward specific goals.

## How to Apply?

### Required Documents

1. **Passport Size Photograph**
   - Depositor: Attested (1 copy)
   - Nominee: Attested by depositor (1 copy)

2. **Identity Documents**
   - NID, Passport, or Birth Registration Certificate
   - Depositor and nominee copies

3. **Minor Account Formalities** (if applicable)
   - Legal guardian documents
   - Operating mandate requirement
   - Guardian signature on form

4. **Active Linked CASA Account**
   - Current or Savings account
   - With Prime Bank
   - Opened before term deposit

5. **Completed Term Deposit Form**
   - Personal Term Deposit opening form
   - Separate form for each deposit
   - All details filled clearly

### Opening Process

1. Visit Prime Bank branch
2. Request "Prime Hasanah Term Deposit" form
3. Decide deposit amount (minimum BDT 10,000)
4. Choose tenor (1-36 months)
5. Provide linked CASA account details
6. Complete form with nominee info
7. Submit all documents with deposit
8. Receive Term Deposit Receipt
9. Profit credited at maturity to linked account

## Auto-Renewal Options

### Option 1: Auto-Renewal With Profit

**What happens:**
- At maturity: Principal + profit = New deposit
- Continues earning interest
- Automatic compounding
- More wealth generation

**Example:**
- 1st TD (12 months): BDT 5,00,000 → BDT 5,41,250
- Auto-renewed: BDT 5,41,250 earns interest again
- 2nd TD (12 months): BDT 5,41,250 → BDT 5,86,826
- Compounded growth: Interest on interest!

---

### Option 2: Auto-Renewal Without Profit (Principal Only)

**What happens:**
- At maturity: Profit credited to account (you receive)
- Principal amount renewed as new deposit
- Income taken out
- Same capital cycles

**Example:**
- 1st TD (12 months): BDT 5,00,000 → Matures
- Profit BDT 41,250 credited (yours to keep)
- Principal BDT 5,00,000 renewed
- Every year: Receive BDT 41,250 interest income

**Use Case:** Regular income stream while capital preserved!

## Investment/Overdraft Facility

### Borrowing Against Term Deposit

- **Maximum Loan:** Up to 90% of deposit amount
- **Interest:** Bank's loan rate
- **Approval:** Quick (2-3 days)
- **Tenure:** Flexible

### How It Works

1. Term Deposit opened: BDT 5,00,000
2. After 3 months: Emergency financial need
3. Available loan: 90% of BDT 5,00,000 = BDT 4,50,000
4. Borrow: Up to BDT 4,50,000
5. TD continues earning
6. At maturity: Profit credited, loan adjusted

---

## Pre-Maturity Encashment

### Early Withdrawal Rules

**If closed before maturity:**
- **Before 1 month:** No profit paid
- **After 1 month:** Profit at reduced rate
- **After 6 months:** Better rate than before 6 months
- **After 12 months:** Nearly full rate

**Recommendation:** Complete tenure for maximum benefit.
