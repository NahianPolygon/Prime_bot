# Prime Hasanah Hajj Scheme

**Tagline:** Journey to Connect, Step by Step

Prime Hasanah Hajj Scheme is a purpose-specific savings scheme designed to help you fulfill your spiritual duty by systematically accumulating funds for Hajj pilgrimage, following Islamic Mudaraba principles.

## Overview

Prime Hasanah Hajj Scheme is a specialized Islamic savings scheme dedicated to helping Bangladeshi Muslims prepare financially for Hajj pilgrimage. With flexible tenors from 1-10 years and monthly installments calculated to reach estimated Hajj costs, it combines religious aspiration with practical financial planning.

## Profit Calculation on Daily Basis

### How Daily Profit Works

**The scheme calculates profit daily, which means:**
- Every day your balance earns profit
- Even weekends and holidays
- Profit compounds on profit
- No waiting for monthly/yearly calculation
- Transparent and Islamic

**Example (Approximate):**
- Monthly deposit: BDT 10,000
- Tenor: 5 years (60 months)
- Daily profit rate: ~0.016% per day
- Month 1 profit: ~BDT 133
- Month 12 profit: ~BDT 1,330 (more, as balance grows)

## FAQs

**Q: What if I miss a monthly payment?**
A: Scheme suspended. Continue payment to resume. Maintain consistency for Hajj readiness.

**Q: Can I increase installment later?**
A: Close current scheme, open new one with higher target and shorter tenor.

**Q: What if I want to go Hajj before maturity?**
A: Encash early, but profit reduced. Better: Take Qard facility to avoid scheme closure.

**Q: Can I extend tenor if I want to postpone Hajj?**
A: No. Keep account; money available for any use. Or open new scheme for extended period.

**Q: Is profit subject to tax?**
A: Yes. Income tax applicable as per laws (~10%). Profit credited after tax deduction.

**Q: What if tenure passed but I didn't do Hajj?**
A: Maturity amount available for use. Can do Hajj anytime. Or restart new scheme.

**Q: Can I do Hajj in a different year than planned?**
A: Yes. Maturity amount available whenever you want to perform Hajj. Your choice.

**Q: Is maturity guaranteed at expected amount?**
A: Profit rate fixed. If you complete tenure, estimated maturity guaranteed.

**Q: Can I open multiple Hajj schemes?**
A: Yes. Each scheme independent. Open multiple for different family members.

**Q: What if I pass away before maturity?**
A: Nominee inherits scheme. Continues with same tenor or withdraws maturity.
