# Prime Hasanah Monthly Income Scheme

**Tagline:** Steady Every Step, Reached New Heights

Prime Hasanah Monthly Income Scheme (PHMIS) is an investment scheme where you deposit a lump sum and earn a fixed monthly income for your selected tenure, following Islamic Mudaraba principles.

---

## Overview

Prime Hasanah Monthly Income Scheme provides a unique investment opportunity where your principal generates a steady monthly income stream (like pension), making it ideal for retirees and income-seeking investors. Operating under Islamic Mudaraba principles, it offers 9% return for 1-year, and 7% for 3-5 year tenors.

---

## What is Monthly Income Scheme?

**PHMIS** provides:
- **Fixed monthly income** (like a pension payment)
- **Principal fully returned** at scheme maturity
- **Islamic Mudaraba principle** for Shariah compliance
- **3 tenure options:** 1, 3, or 5 years
- **Minimum BDT 1 lakh** deposit (multiples)

Perfect for retirees, pensioners, and those needing regular monthly cash flow while preserving capital.

## How to Apply?

### Required Documents

1. **Completed Prime Hasanah Personal Savings Scheme Account Opening Form**
   - Filled with all details
   - Signature required

2. **Active Linked CASA Account**
   - Current or Savings account
   - With Prime Bank
   - Must be opened before scheme

3. **Identity Documents**
   - NID, Passport, or Birth Certificate
   - Applicant and nominee copies

4. **Guardian Documents** (if minor applicant)
   - Parent/legal guardian ID proof
   - Operating mandate documentation

### Opening Process

1. Visit Prime Bank branch
2. Request "Prime Hasanah Monthly Income Scheme" form
3. Choose tenor (1, 3, or 5 years)
4. Specify deposit amount (minimum BDT 1 lakh)
5. Provide linked CASA account details
6. Complete all documentation
7. Submit scheme application
8. Receive confirmation with monthly income details
9. Monthly income starts crediting to linked account

## Pre-Maturity Encashment

### Early Withdrawal Rules

**If scheme is closed before maturity:**
- Monthly income paid to date: **Fully received** (already credited)
- Remaining months' income: **Forfeited**
- Principal amount: **Fully returned** (100%)

**Example:**
- 5-year scheme with BDT 10,00,000
- After 2 years, you close scheme
- You've received: 24 × BDT 5,833.33 = BDT 1,40,000 (yours to keep)
- Remaining 36 months' income: Forfeited
- Principal: BDT 10,00,000 returned (fully)

**Note:** Early encashment not advisable as remaining income forfeited.

## FAQs

**Q: When does monthly income start?**
A: After scheme opening and within 30 days, monthly income starts crediting to your linked account.

**Q: Can I change tenor after opening?**
A: No. Choose carefully at opening. Can open another scheme with different tenor separately.

**Q: Is monthly income subject to tax?**
A: Yes. Income tax applicable as per tax laws (~10% typical). Income credited after tax deduction.

**Q: What if I need money before maturity?**
A: Monthly income already paid is yours. Principal fully returned. Can also take Qard facility.

**Q: Can I close scheme anytime?**
A: Yes, but remaining months' income forfeited. Principal returned. Not advisable to close early.

**Q: Is monthly income guaranteed?**
A: Yes. Fixed rate locked for tenure. Monthly amount fixed from opening day.

**Q: Can multiple schemes be opened?**
A: Yes. Open separate schemes with different amounts or tenors as needed.

**Q: After maturity, what happens?**
A: Principal returned to linked account. Can restart with new scheme or withdraw.

**Q: Is there auto-renewal?**
A: Yes. Principal can be auto-renewed as new scheme without re-applying.

**Q: Can minors open this scheme?**
A: Yes, with parent/legal guardian joint application and operating mandate.
