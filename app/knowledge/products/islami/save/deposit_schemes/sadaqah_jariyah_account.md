# Sadaqah Jariyah Account

**Tagline:** Give Continuously, Earn Eternally

Sadaqah Jariyah Account is a special perpetual waqf-based savings account where customers deposit capital earning 9% annual profit, and all profits are donated yearly to Shariah-approved charitable organizations while principal remains intact eternally.

---

## Overview

Sadaqah Jariyah Account embodies Islamic charity principles by enabling customers to create an enduring charitable legacy. Based on the Islamic concept of Sadaqah Jariyah (continuous charity), customers deposit capital (which never decreases), earn 9% annual profit, and all earnings automatically flow to vetted charitable organizations annually—creating a perpetual benefit stream that continues even after the depositor's lifetime.

Perfect for those seeking to create lasting charitable impact while maintaining their capital wealth.

## The Concept of Sadaqah Jariyah

### Islamic Charity Philosophy

**From Hadith (Sahih Muslim 1631):**
> "When a person dies, his/her deeds come to an end except for three things: Sadaqah Jariyah (ongoing charity), beneficial knowledge, or a virtuous descendant who prays for the deceased."

**Meaning:**
- Continuous charity benefits others lifetime
- Donor rewards continue after death
- Legacy of giving extends eternally
- Family blessed through donation

**Examples of Sadaqah Jariyah:**
- Building a mosque or school
- Sponsoring orphan education
- Establishing healthcare facility
- Planting productive trees
- Creating clean water source
- Teaching valuable skills

## Three Deposit Modes

### Mode 1: Sadaqah Jariyah Fixed

**Deposit Pattern:**
- One-time lump sum deposit
- No recurring deposits
- Capital frozen perpetually
- Immediate income to charity

**Best for:**
- Inheritance windfall

**Deposit Pattern:**

- After tenure: Perpetual donation

**Example:**

- Year 3: Deposit BDT 100,000

## FAQs

**Q: Can I withdraw money from SJA?**
A: No. Account is non-withdrawable. Deposits intentionally locked for perpetual charity.

**Q: What if I need emergency funds?**
A: Keep regular savings account separate. SJA strictly for charitable endowment.

**Q: Is the 9% profit guaranteed?**
A: Yes. Rate locked across all modes and perpetually.

**Q: How is charity distribution decided?**
A: Charity Management Committee selects based on Shariah guidelines and social need.

**Q: Can I choose which organization?**
A: No. Bank selects through committee to ensure fairness and Shariah compliance.

**Q: Can minors open SJA?**
A: Yes, if 18+ can open solo. For minors, guardian opens with their mandate.

**Q: Can I open multiple accounts?**
A: Yes. Multiple SJA accounts possible simultaneously with different modes.

**Q: What happens if I miss installment (flexo)?**
A: System holds amount, manual realization when funds available. Encourage consistency.

**Q: Is SJA profit taxable?**
A: Charitable donations generally tax-exempt. Consult tax professional for specifics.

**Q: Are deposits insured?**
A: Yes. Deposits covered under deposit insurance schemes.

**Q: Can I add more deposits later?**
A: Flexo mode allows additions. Fixed/Installment after term complete: yes.

**Q: How often are charity reports shared?**
A: Annually (January), with quarterly summaries available online.
