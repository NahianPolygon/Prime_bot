# Prime Hasanah Laksma Puron DPS

**Tagline:** Aimed for Sky, Crossed the Limit

Prime Hasanah Laksma Puron DPS is a goal-based monthly installment scheme where you set your target amount and save systematically to reach it, following Islamic Mudaraba principles with flexible 3-5 year tenors.

---

## Overview

Prime Hasanah Laksma Puron DPS empowers you to set your own financial goals and reach them through disciplined monthly savings. Operating under Islamic Mudaraba principles, it offers 6% fixed profit rate across all tenors (3, 4, or 5 years), with transparent monthly installment calculations based on your goal amount.

---

## What is Laksma Puron DPS?

**Laksma Puron** means:
- **Goal-Based Saving** - You set the target
- **Systematic Deposits** - Monthly automatic deductions
- **Custom Installments** - Calculated to reach your goal
- **Flexible Tenors** - 3, 4, or 5 years
- **Fixed Profit** - 6% across all options

Perfect for those with specific financial goals: education, wedding, home, business, etc.

## How to Apply?

### Required Documents

1. **Passport Size Photographs**
   - Depositor: Attested (1 copy)
   - Nominee: Attested by depositor (1 copy)

2. **Identity Documents**
   - NID, Passport, or Birth Registration Certificate
   - Depositor and nominee copies

3. **Minor Account Formalities** (if applicable)
   - Parent/legal guardian consent
   - Formal compliance documentation
   - Mandated guardian signature

4. **Active Linked CASA Account**
   - Current or Savings account
   - With Prime Bank
   - Opened before scheme
   - For automatic deduction

5. **Separate Scheme Forms**
   - Each scheme requires separate form
   - Multiple schemes = Multiple forms
   - All details filled clearly

### Opening Process

1. Visit Prime Bank branch
2. Request "Prime Hasanah Laksma Puron DPS" form
3. Decide goal amount (50K to 1M+)
4. Choose tenor (3, 4, or 5 years)
5. Review monthly installment amount
6. Verify linked CASA account
7. Complete scheme opening form
8. Submit all documents
9. Receive scheme confirmation
10. Automatic monthly deduction starts

## Pre-Maturity Encashment

### Early Withdrawal Rules

**If scheme is closed before maturity:**
- **For deposits held <1 year:** No interest paid
- **For deposits held 1-3 years:** Reduced profit rate (~2-3%)
- **For deposits held >3 years:** Full scheme rate (6%)

**Important:** Early encashment is NOT RECOMMENDED as you lose goal completion.

## FAQs

**Q: What goal amounts can I choose?**
A: Any amount - BDT 50K, 100K, 200K, 300K, 500K, 1M, or higher. Custom goals supported.

**Q: Can I change goal after opening?**
A: No. Goal is fixed. Open separate scheme for different goal.

**Q: What if I can't afford the calculated installment?**
A: Choose longer tenor (5 years instead of 3) to reduce installment.

**Q: Can I open multiple schemes?**
A: Yes! Each goal needs separate scheme. Open as many as needed.

**Q: What if I miss a monthly payment?**
A: Scheme suspended until payment made. Maintain consistency for best results.

**Q: Is the monthly amount fixed throughout?**
A: Yes. Same installment every month for entire tenor.

**Q: Can I increase the goal amount later?**
A: No. Open additional scheme with new goal amount.

**Q: What happens if I close before maturity?**
A: Deposits + reduced profit returned. Goal not achieved. Not recommended.

**Q: Is monthly amount subject to deduction?**
A: No. Full installment amount deducted automatically.

**Q: Can minors open this scheme?**
A: Yes, with parent/legal guardian and minor account formalities.
