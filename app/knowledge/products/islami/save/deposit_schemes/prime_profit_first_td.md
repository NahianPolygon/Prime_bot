# Prime Profit-First TD

**Tagline:** Start with a Win, Earn Profit on Day One

Prime Profit-First TD is a unique fixed deposit account that credits profit on the opening day itself, with the principal amount fully returned at maturity, following Islamic Mudaraba principles.

---

## Overview

Prime Profit-First TD provides an exceptional opportunity to earn interest upfront on your fixed deposit. With advance profit paid on opening day and principal protected until maturity, it combines immediate cash benefit with capital security, perfect for those needing immediate liquidity while locking funds long-term.

---

## What is Profit-First TD?

**Prime Profit-First TD** provides:
- **Advance Profit on Day 1** (paid immediately)
- **12-Month Fixed Tenor** (locked capital)
- **Principal Returned at Maturity** (100% protection)
- **Minimum BDT 1 Lakh** deposit
- **Islamic Mudaraba:** Shariah-compliant

Perfect for investors who want immediate cash benefit from deposits while keeping principal safe.

## How to Apply?

### Required Documents

1. **Completed Personal Fixed Deposit Account Opening Form**
   - All details filled
   - Signature required

2. **Active Linked CASA Account**
   - Current or Savings account
   - With Prime Bank
   - For profit and principal crediting

3. **Identity Documents**
   - NID, Passport, or Birth Certificate
   - Depositor and nominee copies

4. **Guardian Documents** (if minor)
   - Parent/legal guardian documents
   - Operating mandate

### Opening Process

1. Visit Prime Bank branch with BDT 1+ lakh
2. Request "Prime Profit-First TD" form
3. Complete fixed deposit application
4. Provide linked account details
5. Specify nominee information
6. Submit documents
7. Deposit lump sum amount
8. **Day 1:** Receive profit in linked account
9. Receive TD receipt
10. **Month 12:** Principal auto-credited

## Pre-Maturity Encashment Rules

### Early Withdrawal Profit

**If closed before 1 month:**
- No profit paid
- Principal only returned

**If closed after 1 month but before maturity:**
- Profit at Savings Account rate (~2-4%)
- Not full TD rate
- Reduced benefit

**Important:** Upfront profit already paid cannot be refunded. Only principal subject to forfeiture.

## FAQs

**Q: When exactly is profit paid?**
A: On opening day itself (same day, possibly same hour).

**Q: Can I withdraw before 12 months?**
A: Yes, but profit per early encashment rules (reduced).

**Q: Is upfront profit refundable?**
A: No. Profit already paid is yours to keep. Only principal subject to forfeiture if closed early.

**Q: Can I extend after maturity?**
A: Yes. Open new Profit-First TD with principal and/or new amount.

**Q: Is profit guaranteed?**
A: Yes. Rate fixed at opening for 12 months.

**Q: Can minors open this?**
A: Yes, with parent/guardian operating mandate.

**Q: What if I need principal before 12 months?**
A: You can close early, but interest penalty applies. Upfront profit already yours.

**Q: Can I open multiple TDs?**
A: Yes. Open as many as you want with different amounts.

**Q: Is profit taxable?**
A: Yes. Income tax per applicable laws (~10% typical).

**Q: Can I borrow against this?**
A: Yes. Up to 90% of deposit available anytime.
