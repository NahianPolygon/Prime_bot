## Overview

Prime Hasanah Women's Savings Account is a specially designed Islamic account for women age 18+. With tiered profit rates up to 4%, complimentary health insurance coverage (BDT 1 lakh), 50% waiver on locker and investment processing fees, and monthly profit payment, this account celebrates women's financial independence.

- **50% investment processing fee waiver**

Perfect for women seeking competitive returns plus exclusive benefits.

1. **Account Opening Form**
   - Completed personal savings account opening form

2. **Identity Proof**
   - NID, Passport, or Birth Certificate
   - Applicant and nominee

3. **Address Proof**
   - Recent utility bill (Telephone/Electricity/Water)
   - Current residential address

4. **Income/Profession Proof**
   - Salaried: Salary Certificate/LOI/Pay Slip
   - Businessman: Valid trade license
   - Landlord: Ownership documents/rent deed

   - Spouse income (where applicable)

5. **Other Documents**
   - Self-attested passport photo (2 copies)
   - Nominee photo (1 copy, attested)

1. Visit Prime Bank branch
2. Request "Prime Hasanah Women's Savings Account" form
3. Complete application
4. Submit identity and address proofs
5. Provide income/profession documentation
6. Specify nominee details
7. Initial deposit (any amount)
8. Receive account number
9. Activate MyPrime
10. Receive cheque book and debit card

## Insurance Claims Process

### How to Claim

1. **Hospitalization occurs**
   - Inform branch immediately
   - Provide hospital documentation

2. **Claim submission**
   - Fill claim form (provided by bank)
   - Attach hospital bills
   - Attach doctor's reports
   - Attach prescription copies

3. **Processing**
   - Bank reviews documentation
   - Forwards to insurance company
   - Insurance approves/processes

4. **Settlement**
   - Direct payment to hospital OR
   - Reimbursement to account holder

**Contact:** Call 16218 or visit respective branch for assistance

## FAQs

**Q: What if my balance drops below BDT 50,000?**
A: Profit is not calculated for that day. Maintain minimum for daily profit accrual.

**Q: Is 4% profit guaranteed?**
A: It's provisional profit, subject to change per bank policy and Bangladesh Bank guidelines.

**Q: How often is profit paid?**
A: Monthly (12 times yearly), not half-yearly like other accounts.

**Q: Who is eligible for health insurance?**
A: Resident Bangladeshi women age 18-65 at enrollment. Coverage continues to 70 if balance maintained.

**Q: What health conditions does insurance cover?**
A: 10 women-specific conditions plus critical illness (breast cancer, gynecological cancer, heart disease).

**Q: Is there claim limit?**
A: Yes. Overall annual limit BDT 25,000 (except critical illness BDT 100K one-time/year).

**Q: Can I use locker immediately?**
A: Yes. After account opening, locker available (with 50% charge waiver).

**Q: Is this Sharia-compliant?**
A: Yes. Profit calculated per Islamic banking principles, not interest.

**Q: Can I maintain the account after 65?**
A: Yes. Insurance coverage ends at 65 enrollment, but account continues.

**Q: What is the minimum opening amount?**
A: No minimum. Can start with any amount.
