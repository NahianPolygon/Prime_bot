## Overview

Prime Hasanah 50 & Plus Savings Account is a specialized Islamic account for senior citizens aged 50+. With tiered profit rates up to 4.50%, 50% locker charge discount, medical/health benefits at renowned hospitals, free debit card, and free internet banking, this account celebrates the wisdom and experience of seniors.

## How to Apply?

### Required Documents

1. **Account Opening Form**
   - Completed personal account opening form

2. **Identity Proof**
   - NID, Passport, or Birth Certificate
   - Applicant and nominee
   - Age proof documentation

3. **Address Proof**
   - Recent utility bill
   - Residential address

4. **Income/Profession Proof**
   - Pension certificate (if retired)
   - Salary slip (if working)
   - Professional certificate
   - Income source documentation

5. **Other Documents**
   - Self-attested passport photo (2 copies)
   - Nominee photo (1 copy, attested)
   - Tax Return proof/E-TIN (if applicable)
   - Medical checkup report (optional but helpful)

### Opening Process

1. Gather age-related documentation
2. Visit Prime Bank branch
3. Request "50 & Plus Account" form
4. Complete application
5. Submit income/pension proof
6. Provide identity and address proofs
7. Specify medical preferences/health conditions
8. Initial deposit (any amount)
9. Receive account number
10. Activate MyPrime
11. Register for health benefits
