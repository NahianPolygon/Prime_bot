## How to Apply?

### Required Documents

1. **Account Opening Form**
   - Completed personal account opening form
   - Business details section

2. **Identity Proof**
   - NID or Passport of applicant(s)
   - For all partners (if partnership)

3. **Address Proof**
   - Recent utility bill (Telephone/Electricity/Water)
   - Residential address
   - Business address (if different)

4. **Income/Profession Proof**
   - Salaried: Salary Certificate/LOI/Pay Slip
   - Businessman: Valid trade license
   - Landlord: Ownership documents/rent deed
   - Self-employed: Professional certificate
   - Professional: Association membership

5. **Business Documentation** (if applicable)
   - Trade license copy
   - Business registration
   - Partnership deed (if partnership)
   - Professional certificate

6. **Other Documents**
   - Self-attested passport photo (2 copies)
   - Nominee photo (1 copy, attested)
   - Tax Return proof/E-TIN (if applicable)
   - FATCA form

### Opening Process

1. Visit Prime Bank branch
2. Request "Prime Hasanah Current Account" form
3. Explain business/professional operations
4. Complete account opening form
5. Submit all required documentation
6. Initial deposit: BDT 1,000
7. Receive account number
8. Set up MyPrime (recommended)
9. Receive debit card

## FAQs

**Q: Is profit paid on current account?**
A: No. Current accounts are non-profit bearing by design.

**Q: Are there transaction limits?**
A: No. Unlimited transactions within available balance.

**Q: What if balance goes to zero?**
A: Allowed. No minimum balance requirement for operations.

**Q: Can I use cheque book?**
A: Yes. Cheque facility available on request.

**Q: Is overdraft available?**
A: Not standard. Check with branch for special arrangements.

**Q: Can partnership open this account?**
A: Yes. With partnership deed and all partner documentation.

**Q: Is this Sharia-compliant?**
A: Yes. Zero-profit/loss structure ensures Sharia compliance.

**Q: What's the minimum opening amount?**
A: Only BDT 1,000. Very affordable for startups.

**Q: Can I convert to savings account?**
A: Yes. Request branch for account conversion.

**Q: Are international transactions possible?**
A: Limited. CASA account restrictions apply per regulations.
