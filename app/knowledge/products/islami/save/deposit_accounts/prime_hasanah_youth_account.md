Prime Hasanah Youth Account is an Islamic savings account designed for young adults aged 18-25. ## How to Apply?

### Required Documents

1. **Account Opening Form**
   - Completed personal savings account opening form
   - Self-declaration section

2. **Identity Proof**
   - NID, Passport, or Birth Certificate
   - Original must be produced at opening
   - Copy retained by bank

3. **Address Proof**
   - Recent utility bill (Telephone/Electricity/Water)
   - Current residential address
   - Proof in applicant's name preferred

4. **Student/Professional Proof**
   - Valid Student ID (if student)
   - School/College/University certificate
   - Employment ID (if working)
   - Job letter (if employed)

5. **Income Proof (if applicable)**
   - Internship certificate
   - Part-time work letter
   - Freelance portfolio proof
   - Family income documentation

6. **Other Documents**
   - Self-attested passport photo (2 copies)
   - Nominee photo (1 copy, attested by applicant)
   - Tax Return proof/E-TIN (if applicable)

### Opening Process

1. Visit Prime Bank branch
2. Request "Prime Hasanah Youth Account" form
3. Complete application with personal details
4. Provide identity and address proofs
5. Submit student/professional documentation
6. Specify nominee details
7. Make initial deposit (any amount)
8. Receive account number
9. Activate MyPrime online banking
10. Receive debit card (2-3 working days)

## FAQs

**Q: I'm 26 years old. Can I open this account?**
A: No. This account is for ages 18-25. Once you turn 25, you can maintain the account but cannot open new accounts in this category.

**Q: Is the 4% profit guaranteed?**
A: It's provisional profit, subject to change by Prime Bank and Bangladesh Bank guidelines.

**Q: When is profit paid?**
A: Twice yearly, automatically credited at 6-month intervals.

**Q: Can I withdraw profit anytime?**
A: Yes. Profit credited to account and available for withdrawal anytime.

**Q: Is this Sharia-compliant?**
A: Yes. Returns calculated as provisional profit per Islamic banking principles, not interest.

**Q: Can I keep the account after 25?**
A: Yes. Account continues but you cannot open new Youth Accounts.

**Q: What's the minimum balance?**
A: No minimum balance requirement. Can operate with any amount.

**Q: Can I upgrade to a different account?**
A: Yes. As you progress professionally, other Prime Hasanah accounts available.

**Q: Is debit card unlimited?**
A: Yes. Unlimited transactions as per available balance. Daily limits per bank policy.

**Q: Are there transaction limits?**
A: Per online banking security. Check specific limits in MyPrime or contact branch.
