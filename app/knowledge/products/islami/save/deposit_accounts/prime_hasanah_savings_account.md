## Profit Rates & Structure

### Daily Profit Mechanism

**Profit calculation:**
- 2% annual provisional rate
- Calculated on daily balance
- Credited half-yearly (every 6 months)
- Minimum balance BDT 10,000 for eligibility

### Profit Eligibility Rules

| Daily Balance | Profit Status |
|---------------|---------------|
| Below BDT 10,000 | No profit that day |
| BDT 10,000+ | Profit accrues that day |
| BDT 10,000+ (consistent) | Regular profit accumulation |

## How to Apply?

### Required Documents

1. **Account Opening Form**
   - Completed personal savings account opening form

2. **Identity Proof**
   - NID, Passport, or Birth Certificate
   - Applicant and nominee

3. **Address Proof**
   - Recent utility bill (Telephone/Electricity/Water)
   - Current residential address

4. **Income/Profession Proof**
   - Salaried: Salary Certificate/LOI/Pay Slip/Job ID
   - Businessman: Valid trade license copy
   - Landlord: Ownership documents/rent receipt
   - Self-employed: Professional certificate
   - Spouse income (where applicable)

5. **Other Documents**
   - Self-attested passport photo (2 copies)
   - Nominee photo (1 copy, attested)
   - Tax Return proof/E-TIN (if applicable)
   - FATCA form (if applicable)

### Opening Process

1. Visit Prime Bank branch
2. Request "Prime Hasanah Savings Account" form
3. Complete application
4. Submit identity and address proofs
5. Provide income/profession documentation
6. Specify nominee information
7. Initial deposit: BDT 500 (minimum)
8. Receive account number
9. Set up MyPrime (optional)
10. Receive cheque book and debit card

## FAQs

**Q: What if my balance falls below BDT 10,000?**
A: You can still maintain the account. Profit accrues only when balance is BDT 10,000+ on that day.

**Q: Is 2% profit guaranteed?**
A: It's provisional profit, subject to change per bank policy and Bangladesh Bank directives.

**Q: When is profit paid?**
A: Twice yearly, automatically credited at 6-month intervals.

**Q: Can I withdraw anytime?**
A: Yes. No withdrawal restrictions. Can withdraw entire balance anytime.

**Q: Is this Sharia-compliant?**
A: Yes. Returns as provisional profit per Islamic banking principles, not interest.

**Q: Can minors operate accounts?**
A: Only with guardian operating mandate. Guardian controls transactions.

**Q: What's the initial deposit?**
A: Only BDT 500. Very affordable entry point.

**Q: Are there monthly charges?**
A: No. Zero account maintenance fee. Pure profit accumulation.

**Q: Can I maintain below BDT 10,000?**
A: Yes, but profit only accrues when balance reaches BDT 10,000+.

**Q: Can account be upgraded later?**
A: Yes. As circumstances change, other Prime Hasanah accounts available.
