### Insurance Details

- **Minimum monthly balance:** BDT 100,000 (for coverage eligibility)
- **Age criteria:** 18-64 years at enrollment
- **Extended coverage:** Continues to age 65 if balance maintained
- **Automatic enrollment:** Upon account opening
- **No cost:** Completely complimentary
- **Family benefit:** Paid to nominee upon death

### Claims Process

1. Inform bank immediately upon death
2. Provide death certificate
3. Bank forwards to insurance company
4. Insurance processes claim
5. Payment to nominee/beneficiary
6. Contact branch or call 16218 for assistance

## How to Apply?

### Required Documents

1. **Account Opening Form**
   - Completed personal savings account opening form

2. **Identity Proof**
   - NID, Passport, or Birth Certificate
   - Applicant and nominee
   - For minors: Guardian ID

3. **Address Proof**
   - Recent utility bill (Telephone/Electricity/Water)
   - Current residential address

4. **Income/Profession Proof**
   - Salaried: Salary Certificate/LOI/Pay Slip/Job ID
   - Businessman: Valid trade license
   - Landlord: Ownership documents/rent receipt
   - Self-employed: Professional certificate
   - Spouse income (where applicable)

5. **Other Documents**
   - Self-attested passport photo (2 copies)
   - Nominee photo (1 copy, attested)
   - Tax Return proof/E-TIN (if applicable)

### Opening Process

1. Visit Prime Bank branch
2. Request "Prime Hasanah One Savings Account" form
3. Complete application with personal details
4. Provide identity and address proofs
5. Submit income/profession documentation
6. Specify nominee information
7. Initial deposit: BDT 500 (minimum)
8. Receive account number
9. Activate MyPrime (recommended)
10. Receive cheque book and debit card
11. Life insurance automatically enrolled

## FAQs

**Q: What if my balance falls below BDT 1 lakh?**
A: Account continues. Profit not earned for days balance is below 1L. But account operations normal.

**Q: Is 4% profit guaranteed?**
A: It's provisional profit, subject to change per bank policy and Bangladesh Bank guidelines.

**Q: How often is profit paid?**
A: Monthly (12 times yearly). Better frequency than half-yearly accounts.

**Q: Can minors open this account?**
A: Yes. With parent/legal guardian operating mandate until age 18.

**Q: Is insurance really free?**
A: Yes. Complimentary life insurance. No premium payment whatsoever.

**Q: What's the insurance claim process?**
A: Inform bank, provide death certificate, bank processes through insurer, payment to nominee.

**Q: Is this Sharia-compliant?**
A: Yes. All returns as provisional profit per Islamic banking principles, not interest.

**Q: Can I maintain account after turning 65?**
A: Yes. Insurance coverage ends at 65 enrollment but account continues indefinitely.

**Q: Why is intercity/online transfer free?**
A: Bank's customer-friendly policy. No charge for geographical flexibility.

**Q: Can I upgrade to higher balance tier?**
A: Yes. Profit automatically recalculates as balance grows. Tiered benefit increases.
