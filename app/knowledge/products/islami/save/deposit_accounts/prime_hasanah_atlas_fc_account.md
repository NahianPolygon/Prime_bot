## Overview

Prime Hasanah Atlas FC Account is a specialized Islamic multi-currency foreign current account designed for Bangladeshi maritime workers and crew members. With multi-currency support (USD, GBP, Euro, JPY), unlimited transactions, seamless remittance capability, local currency withdrawal through linked account, international debit card, and Wage Earners' Bond eligibility, this account serves the unique needs of maritime professionals.

## Wage Earners' Bond Opportunity

### What is Wage Earners' Bond?

- **Issuer:** Government of Bangladesh (Ministry of Finance)
- **Purpose:** Encourage overseas earnings repatriation
- **Eligible:** Wage earners (including mariners)
- **Currency:** USD typically
- **Returns:** Higher than regular deposits
- **Term:** 2-5 years typically
- **Capital Protection:** Guaranteed by government

### Wage Earners' Bond Benefits

| Feature | Benefit |
|---------|---------|
| **Higher interest** | 6-8% typically (vs 1-3% regular deposits) |
| **Government guarantee** | 100% capital protection |
| **Tax benefit** | Potential tax exemption |
| **Liquidity** | Can be encashed before maturity (with penalty) |
| **Heritage** | Legacy for family |

### How to Invest in Wage Earners' Bond

1. Open Atlas FC Account (if not existing)
2. Accumulate USD in account
3. Contact bank for bond application
4. Select tenor (2-5 years)
5. Submit documentation
6. Bond issued
7. Profit credited periodically
8. At maturity: Principal + interest returned

## NFCD (Non-Resident Foreign Currency Deposit)

### What is NFCD?

- **Type:** Fixed deposit in foreign currency
- **Applicant:** Non-residents and overseas workers
- **Currency:** USD, GBP, EUR, JPY
- **Term:** Flexible (typically 3 months to 5 years)
- **Returns:** Higher than regular deposits
- **Capital:** 100% protected

### NFCD Advantages

- **Higher interest rates** (typically 4-6%)
- **Flexible tenor** options
- **Foreign currency retention** (no forced conversion)
- **Capital guaranteed**
- **Mariner-friendly** terms
- **Competitive rates** vs regular banks

## How to Apply?

### Required Documents (For Mariner)

1. **Account Opening Form**
   - Completed personal account opening form

2. **Identity Proof**
   - Valid Passport (primary)
   - NID or Birth Certificate (alternative)
   - Applicant and nominee

3. **Address Proof**
   - Recent utility bill
   - Residential address
   - Local address documentation

4. **Professional Proof**
   - Valid passport with visa
   - **Mariner's Discharge Book** (official credential)
   - Letter from local agent confirming employment
   - Work contract for current vessel
   - Letter from employer/shipping company
   - Job ID or employment certificate

5. **For Nominee**
   - Identity proof (NID/Passport)
   - Photograph (1 copy, attested)
   - Relationship proof (marriage cert, birth cert, etc.)

6. **Other Documents**
   - Self-attested passport photo (2 copies)

### Opening Process

1. Gather all documentation
2. Visit Prime Bank AD branch
3. Request "Atlas FC Account" form
4. Complete application
5. Explain maritime employment
6. Submit Discharge Book + employment letter
7. Provide passport with visa
8. Address verification
9. Specify currencies needed
10. Receive account number
11. Activate MyPrime (optional)
12. International card ordered
13. Linked BDT account created
