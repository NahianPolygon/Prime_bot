## Overview

Prime Hasanah RFCD (Resident Foreign Currency Deposit) Account is a multi-currency Islamic savings account for international travelers and expatriates. With 1% provisional profit on FCY balances, Visa Signature debit card, multi-currency deposit facility (USD, GBP, Euro), cash deposit up to USD 10,000 tax-free per visit, and no account maintenance fee, this account is perfect for managing foreign currency conveniently.

---

## What is RFCD Account?

**RFCD Account** provides:
- **1% provisional profit** p.a. on FCY balance
- **Monthly profit payment** (paid monthly)
- **Multi-currency support:** USD, GBP, Euro
- **Visa Signature debit card** (USD account)
- **Hassle-free cash deposits:** Up to USD 10,000 per return
- **BDT withdrawal:** Convert to local currency anytime
- **No account maintenance fee**
- **No minimum balance requirement**

Perfect for frequent travelers, expatriates, and those managing international finances.

## How to Apply?

### Required Documents

1. **Account Opening Form**
   - Completed RFCD account opening form

2. **Identity Proof**
   - NID, Passport, or Birth Certificate
   - Applicant and nominee

3. **Address Proof**
   - Recent utility bill
   - Residential address
   - Local address confirmation

4. **Passport Documentation**
   - Valid passport copy
   - Immigration arrival stamp (recent)
   - Proof of international travel
   - Visa/travel history

5. **FMJ Form** (if applicable)
   - For deposits above USD 10,000
   - Declaration of foreign currency amount
   - Source documentation
   - Customs compliance

6. **Other Documents**
   - Self-attested passport photo (2 copies)
   - Nominee photo (1 copy, attested)
   - Tax Return proof/E-TIN (if applicable)

### Opening Process

1. Visit Prime Bank branch
2. Request "RFCD Account" form
3. Bring foreign currency cash (or not)
4. Complete account opening form
5. Submit passport with immigration stamp
6. Provide identity/address proofs
7. If USD 10K+: Submit FMJ form
8. Deposit foreign currency (if bringing)
9. Receive account number
10. Activate MyPrime
11. Receive Visa Signature card (2-3 weeks)

## FAQs

**Q: What currencies can I hold?**
A: USD, GBP, EUR primarily. Other currencies per bank approval.

**Q: Is 1% profit guaranteed?**
A: It's provisional profit, subject to change per bank policy and Bangladesh Bank guidelines.

**Q: Can I use debit card in Bangladesh?**
A: No. Card works only abroad at ATM/POS. Cannot use locally in Bangladesh.

**Q: How much can I deposit without declaration?**
A: Up to USD 10,000 per arrival. Above requires FMJ form.

**Q: Can I withdraw USD cash anytime?**
A: Yes. Visit branch, request withdrawal, receive cash.

**Q: How do I convert USD to BDT?**
A: Visit Prime Bank branch, request conversion, get BDT at prevailing rate.

**Q: Is profit paid monthly?**
A: Yes. Monthly payments (12 times yearly).

**Q: Is this Sharia-compliant?**
A: Yes. Profit as per Islamic banking principles, not interest.

**Q: Can I have multiple RFCD accounts?**
A: Clarify with branch. Generally one primary recommended.

**Q: What happens if I stop traveling?**
A: Account continues. Can maintain FCY savings indefinitely.
