# Prime Hasanah Teacher's Account

**Tagline:** A Savings Guide for the Teachers

Prime Hasanah Teacher's Account is designed to provide the right financial solution for those who always guide us towards the right direction.

## Overview

Prime Hasanah Teacher's Account is a specialized Islamic account for educators (teachers, lecturers, administrators). With up to 5% provisional profit, tiered by balance, complimentary life insurance coverage (up to BDT 3 lakh), 50% locker charge discount, 50% investment processing fee waiver, and monthly profit payment, this account celebrates the teaching profession.

## How to Apply?

### Required Documents

1. **Account Opening Form**
   - Completed personal account opening form

2. **Identity Proof**
   - NID, Passport, or Birth Certificate
   - Applicant and nominee

3. **Address Proof**
   - Recent utility bill
   - Residential address

4. **Income/Profession Proof**
   - **Salary Certificate** from school/college
   - **LOI (Letter of Intent)** from institution
   - **Pay Slip** (recent, 1-2 months)
   - **Job ID** from educational institution
   - All showing educator status

5. **Professional Proof**
   - School/college ID card
   - Appointment letter
   - Professional teaching certificate (if applicable)
   - Verification from institution

6. **Other Documents**
   - Self-attested passport photo (2 copies)
   - Nominee photo (1 copy, attested)
   - Tax Return proof/E-TIN (if applicable)

### Opening Process

1. Gather employment documents
2. Visit Prime Bank branch
3. Request "Teacher's Account" form
4. Complete application
5. Submit salary certificate/appointment letter
6. Provide identity and address proofs
7. Initial deposit (any amount)
8. Receive account number
9. Activate MyPrime
10. Receive cheque book and debit card

## FAQs

**Q: What counts as proof of being a teacher?**
A: Salary certificate, appointment letter, pay slip, school ID, or institutional letter from employer suffices.

**Q: Can I open account after retirement?**
A: Yes. Retired teachers can open (insurance coverage available until previously enrolled).

**Q: Is 5% profit guaranteed?**
A: It's provisional profit, subject to change per bank policy and Bangladesh Bank guidelines.

**Q: How often is profit paid?**
A: Monthly (12 times yearly), not half-yearly.

**Q: Can my family claim insurance if something happens?**
A: Yes. Nominee designated at account opening receives insurance benefit.

**Q: Is this Sharia-compliant?**
A: Yes. Profit as per Islamic banking principles, not conventional interest.

**Q: What if my balance fluctuates monthly?**
A: Profit calculated on monthly average balance for profit tier determination.

**Q: Can I use locker immediately?**
A: Yes. Locker available after account opening with 50% discount on regular charges.

**Q: What happens if I change school?**
A: Account continues. Just update employer details for salary verification if needed.

**Q: Is there minimum balance to maintain?**
A: No minimum. Can operate with any balance (but lower balance = lower profit tier).
