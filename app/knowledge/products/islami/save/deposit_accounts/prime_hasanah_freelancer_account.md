## Overview

Prime Hasanah Freelancer Account is a bundled Islamic account solution for freelancers earning foreign currency (FCY) through outsourcing. With USD zero-balance account, 35% FCY retention per Bangladesh Bank ERQ limit, international debit card, and complementary insurance, this account is tailored for digital entrepreneurs.

---

## What is Prime Hasanah Freelancer Account?

**Freelancer Bundle** includes:
- **Prime Hasanah Freelancer ERQ Account** (Foreign Currency: USD/other)
- **Prime Hasanah One Savings Account** (Bangladeshi Taka: BDT)
- **Zero balance** on USD account
- **35% FCY retention** (up to Bangladesh Bank ERQ limit)
- **Free international debit card** (USD account)
- **Complementary life insurance** on BDT account
- **Seamless multi-currency** transactions
- **Dedicated service desk** at selected branches

Perfect for freelancers managing income across currencies.

## ERQ Account Details

### Exporters' Retention Quota (ERQ)

**What is ERQ?**
- Special account for exporters (including freelancers)
- Retain portion of foreign earnings
- In foreign currency (non-profit bearing)
- Per Bangladesh Bank guidelines

**ERQ Limit:**
- **Current limit:** 35% of inward remittance
- **Subject to:** Bangladesh Bank directive
- **May change:** As per regulatory updates
- **Example:** BDT 100K earned → Keep USD equivalent of BDT 35K max

## Linked CASA Account Requirement

### Why Linked Account is Required

1. **ERQ accounts must be linked** to BDT savings/current account
2. **Automatic crediting:** 65% of remittance goes to BDT account
3. **Operational mandate:** Cannot operate separately
4. **Cannot close:** Linked BDT account cannot be closed without ERQ

### Linked Account Benefits

- **Automatic conversion:** 65% flows here
- **BDT profit:** Earn profit on balance
- **Insurance:** Complementary coverage
- **Cheque book:** Access to cheques
- **Debit card:** Local currency card

## Account Opening Process

### Step-by-Step

1. **Existing CASA Account**
   - Must have Prime Hasanah One Savings/Current account
   - Open this first if needed

2. **Visit Prime Bank AD Branch**
   - Take to any authorized dealer branch
   - Request "Freelancer ERQ Account" form

3. **Submit Documentation**
   - Completed account opening form
   - Identity proof (NID/Passport/Birth Certificate)
   - Address proof (utility bill)
   - Freelance income proof (one of accepted documents)
   - Passport size photos (2 copies)
   - Tax proof if applicable

4. **Account Activation**
   - Linked to existing CASA account
   - Zero balance USD account opened
   - International debit card ordered (2-3 days)

5. **Receive Materials**
   - Account number for USD account
   - Debit card arrives
   - Documentation for record

6. **Start Receiving Remittances**
   - Direct FCY transfer possible
   - Or through Prime Exchange
   - 35% retained, 65% to linked BDT

## FAQs

**Q: Can I open ERQ account without Prime Bank CASA account?**
A: No. Must have linked Prime Hasanah One Savings/Current account first.

**Q: What if I earn more than 35% ERQ limit?**
A: Excess automatically credited to linked BDT account at prevailing rate.

**Q: Can I use international card in Bangladesh?**
A: No. Card works only abroad at ATM/POS. Cannot use in Bangladesh locally.

**Q: How often is 65% converted to BDT?**
A: Upon crediting to your account. Automatic conversion at rate of that day.

**Q: Can I hold multiple ERQ accounts?**
A: No. Only one ERQ account allowed per customer.

**Q: Can NRB or foreigner open this?**
A: No. Only Resident Bangladeshi freelancers can open.

**Q: Can I convert USD back to ERQ?**
A: You can request conversion at AD Branch, but 35% limit still applies.

**Q: Is there profit on USD account?**
A: No. ERQ account is non-profit bearing per regulations.

**Q: What happens if I stop freelancing?**
A: You can maintain account. ERQ functionality applies only when remittances credited.

**Q: How do I receive remittances?**
A: Through Prime Exchange or direct bank transfer to account number.
