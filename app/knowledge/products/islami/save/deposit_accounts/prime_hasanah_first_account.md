## Overview

Prime Hasanah First Account is a specialized Islamic savings account designed for students under 18 years old. With Sharia-compliant returns (5% provisional profit), daily profit-bearing facility, and parental/guardian management, this account is the perfect first banking experience for young learners.

---

## What is Prime Hasanah First Account?

**Hasanah First Account** provides:
- **5% provisional profit** on daily balance
- **Daily profit-bearing facility** (Sharia-compliant)
- **Half-yearly profit payment** (semi-annual)
- **Free debit card and cheque book** for guardian use
- **No minimum balance requirement**
- **Initial deposit:** BDT 100 only

Perfect for parents/guardians building financial literacy for their children while keeping funds safe and earning Sharia-compliant returns.

## How to Apply?

### Required Documents

1. **Account Opening Form**
   - Completed personal savings account opening form
   - Student and guardian information

2. **Identity Proof (Student)**
   - NID, Passport, or Birth Certificate
   - Copied (not original)

3. **Identity Proof (Parent/Guardian)**
   - NID or Passport
   - Attested copy

4. **Address Proof Document**
   - Recent utility bill (Telephone/Electricity/Water)
   - Guardian's residential address
   - Copy not required original

5. **Income/Profession Proof (Guardian)**
   - Salary Certificate/LOI/Pay Slip
   - Trade License (if businessman)
   - Ownership documents (if landlord)
   - Professional certificate (if self-employed)

6. **School Documentation (Student)**
   - School ID or school certificate
   - Latest tuition fee payment slip
   - Proof of enrollment

7. **Other Documents**
   - Student passport photo (2 copies, attested)
   - Guardian passport photo (1 copy)
   - Nominee photo (1 copy, attested)
   - Tax Return Submission Proof/E-TIN (if applicable)

### Opening Process

1. Visit Prime Bank branch with guardian and student
2. Request "Prime Hasanah First Account" form
3. Complete form with both student and guardian details
4. Provide identity proofs and address verification
5. Submit school documentation
6. Initial deposit: BDT 100 (minimum)
7. Receive account number and debit card
8. Set up MyPrime online banking
9. Receive cheque book after verification

## FAQs

**Q: At what age can my child open this account?**
A: Any age under 18 years. Guardian manages account until child turns 18.

**Q: What's the initial deposit?**
A: Only BDT 100 minimum. Very affordable entry point.

**Q: Is the 5% profit guaranteed?**
A: It's provisional profit, subject to change by bank per market conditions and Bangladesh Bank guidelines.

**Q: How often is profit paid?**
A: Twice yearly (every 6 months). Automatic credit to account.

**Q: Can my child make withdrawals?**
A: With guardian's authorization only. Guardian controls transactions until age 18.

**Q: Is this account Sharia-compliant?**
A: Yes. All returns are calculated as provisional profit (not interest) per Islamic banking principles.

**Q: What happens when child turns 18?**
A: Account automatically converts to adult account. Child takes full control.

**Q: Can I add money regularly?**
A: Yes. Any amount anytime. Encourage regular deposits for savings habit.

**Q: Are there monthly charges?**
A: No. Zero maintenance fee. Pure profit accumulation.

**Q: Can guardian be changed?**
A: Yes. Through formal request and documentation update at branch.
