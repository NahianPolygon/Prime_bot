## FAQs

**Q: What makes Platinum different from Gold?**
A: Higher credit limit (BDT 10 lac vs 7 lac), premium BOGO dining, higher insurance (BDT 10 lac vs 5 lac), LoungeKey access, airport welcome service.

**Q: Is Visa Hasanah truly Riba-free?**
A: Yes. Ujrah-based (permissible service charges). No interest ever applied. Shariah Supervisory approved.

**Q: Can I use BOGO at any restaurant?**
A: Only at 6 specified premium partners: Westin, InterContinental, Le Meridien, Radisson Blu, Sheraton, Pan Pacific.

**Q: How does LoungeKey fee waiver work?**
A: Spend USD 500 in travel transactions during your travel period. Contact 16218 with spend proof. Waiver applied.

**Q: Can I transfer my balance from another card?**
A: Yes. Balance transfer available with 1-2% Ujrah charge (not interest).

**Q: What if I miss a payment?**
A: Late charge 2% monthly. Strongly advised to setup standing instruction for automatic payment.

**Q: Are reward points taxable?**
A: Generally, rewards are not taxable as they're marketing incentives. Consult tax advisor for specific situation.

**Q: Can I increase limit anytime?**
A: Yes. Request after 6 months of good payment. Automatic annual review conducted.

**Q: What's the minimum payment?**
A: Minimum 5% of outstanding or BDT 5,000, whichever is higher.

**Q: Is travel insurance included?**
A: Yes, automatic travel insurance included. Covers emergency medical, trip cancellation, luggage loss.
