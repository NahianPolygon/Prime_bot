## Overview

Prime Savings Account is designed for all Bangladeshi citizens, regardless of age or employment status. It's a flexible savings solution that allows you to earn interest on deposits while maintaining complete control over your funds with free internet banking.

---

## Eligibility

- **Age:** Any age (minors require parental/legal guardian consent)
- **Nationality:** Resident Bangladeshi Citizen
- **Status:** Any employment status
- **For Minors:** Account can be opened:
  - Singly with parental/legal guardian operating mandate, OR
  - Jointly with parental/legal guardian with parental/legal guardian operating mandate

## How to Apply?

### Required Documents

1. **Completed Account Opening Form**
   - Available at any Prime Bank branch

2. **Identity Proof Document**
   - NID, OR
   - Passport, OR
   - Birth Certificate
   - Required for applicant(s) and nominee(s)

3. **Address Proof Document**
   - Recent copy of utility bill (Telephone/Electricity/Water bill, etc.)

4. **Income/Profession Proof Document**
   - **For Salaried Employees:**
     - Salary Certificate, LOI (Letter of Intention), OR
     - Pay Slip, OR
     - Job ID
   
   - **For Business Owners:**
     - Valid trade license copy
   
   - **For Property Owners:**
     - Ownership documents, OR
     - Rent receipt/rental deed
   
   - **For Self-Employed:**
     - Professional certificate
   
   - **For Spouse Income:**
     - Supporting income proof document (where applicable)

5. **Other Documents**
   - Self-attested recent passport-size photo (2 copies) of applicant(s)
   - 1 photo of nominee(s) attested by account holder
   - Proof of Submission of Tax Return (PSR) or E-TIN certificate (where applicable)
   - FATCA (Foreign Account Tax Compliance Act) form

### Opening Process

1. Visit nearest Prime Bank branch
2. Submit completed account opening form with all documents
3. Bank verifies documents and eligibility
4. Activate account with initial deposit
5. Receive debit card and internet banking credentials

## FAQs

**Q: What is the minimum balance to open an account?**
A: Only BDT 500. You don't need BDT 10,000 to open—that's the minimum to earn interest.

**Q: Can a minor open this account alone?**
A: No, minors must have parental/legal guardian consent and operating mandate.

**Q: What if my balance falls below BDT 10,000?**
A: Interest won't be credited for that period, but you can still use the account.

**Q: How often is interest paid?**
A: Interest is paid half-yearly (every 6 months).

**Q: Can I close the account anytime?**
A: Yes, you can close the account anytime. Visit your branch or request online.

**Q: What if the interest rate changes?**
A: The bank will notify you in advance of any interest rate changes. You can compare and decide accordingly.

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**
A: Yes, you can have multiple Savings Accounts.

**Q: Is FATCA form mandatory?**
A: Yes, if you have foreign income or assets. It's required by international regulations.
