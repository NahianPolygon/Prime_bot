## How to Apply?

### Required Documents

1. **Completed Account Opening Form**
   - Available at any Prime Bank branch

2. **Identity Proof Document**
   - NID, OR
   - Passport, OR
   - Birth Certificate
   - Required for applicant(s) and nominee(s)

3. **Address Proof Document**
   - Recent copy of utility bill (Telephone/Electricity/Water bill, etc.)

4. **Income/Profession Proof Document**
   - **For Salaried Employees:**
     - Salary Certificate, OR
     - Letter of Intent (LOI), OR
     - Pay Slip, OR
     - Job ID
   
   - **For Business Owners:**
     - Valid trade license copy
     - Business registration document
   
   - **For Property Owners/Landlords:**
     - Ownership documents, OR
     - Rent receipt, OR
     - Rental deed
   
   - **For Self-Employed:**
     - Professional certificate
     - Business license or registration
   
   - **Spouse Income** (where applicable):
     - Supporting income proof document

5. **Other Documents**
   - Self-attested recent passport-size photo (2 copies) of applicant(s)
   - 1 photo of nominee(s) attested by account holder
   - Proof of Submission of Tax Return (PSR) or E-TIN Certificate (where applicable)
   - FATCA (Foreign Account Tax Compliance Act) form

### Opening Process

1. Visit nearest Prime Bank branch
2. Submit completed application with all documents
3. Bank verifies documents and eligibility
4. Deposit initial amount (minimum BDT 1,000)
5. Account activation and debit card issuance
6. Receive internet banking credentials

## FAQs

**Q: Is there an interest rate on Current Account?**
A: No. Current Accounts don't earn interest. If you want to earn interest, consider Prime Savings Account.

**Q: What is the minimum balance requirement?**
A: There's no minimum balance requirement to maintain the account.

**Q: Can I have both Savings and Current Account?**
A: Yes, you can have multiple accounts at the same time.

**Q: What if my business is growing rapidly?**
A: Current Account grows with your business. No transaction limits means no restrictions as you scale.

**Q: Can I get a cheque book?**
A: Yes, cheque books are available. Contact your branch for details.

**Q: Is there a limit on how much I can deposit per day?**
A: No limit. Deposit any amount anytime.

**Q: Can I open a joint account?**
A: Yes, you can have multiple account holders with operating mandates.

**Q: How do I monitor business transactions?**
A: Use MyPrime Internet Banking to view real-time transactions, or visit your branch for detailed statements.

**Q: What is the FATCA form?**
A: FATCA is a regulatory requirement for accounts. It confirms your tax residency status. Your bank will provide the form.

**Q: How long does account opening take?**
A: Typically 24-48 hours from document submission.
