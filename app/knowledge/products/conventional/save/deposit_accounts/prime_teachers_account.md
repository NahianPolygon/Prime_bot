# Prime Teacher's Savings Account

**Tagline:** A Savings Guide for the Teachers

Prime Teacher's Savings Account is designed to provide the right financial solution for those who always guide us towards the right direction.

## Overview

Prime Teacher's Savings Account is specially designed for educators (teachers, college lecturers, university professors) to reward their dedication with attractive interest rates, life insurance coverage, and exclusive benefits including locker discounts and loan processing fee waivers.

---

## How to Apply?

### Required Documents

1. **Completed Account Opening Form**
   - Available at any Prime Bank branch

2. **Identity Proof Document**
   - NID, OR
   - Passport, OR
   - Birth Certificate
   - Required for applicant(s) and nominee(s)

3. **Address Proof Document**
   - Recent copy of utility bill (Telephone/Electricity/Water bill, etc.)

4. **Income/Profession Proof Document**
   - Salary Certificate, OR
   - Letter of Intent (LOI), OR
   - Recent Pay Slip, OR
   - Job ID
   - From your school/college/university

5. **Other Documents**
   - Self-attested recent passport-size photo (2 copies) of applicant(s)
   - 1 photo of nominee(s) attested by account holder
   - Proof of Submission of Tax Return (PSR) or E-TIN Certificate (where applicable)

### Opening Process

1. Visit nearest Prime Bank branch
2. Bring employer verification letter (salary certificate or LOI)
3. Submit Account Opening Form with all documents
4. Bank verifies your teaching profession
5. Account activated on same day
6. Receive debit card and cheque book within 24 hours

## FAQs

**Q: What salary slip do I need?**
A: Latest salary slip from your school/college/university. Should be within 1 month old.

**Q: Can retired teachers open this account?**
A: No. This account is for active, employed teachers only.

**Q: How much interest will I earn?**
A: Depends on your balance. Minimum 0% (below BDT 50K), maximum 5% (above BDT 20 lac).

**Q: When is interest paid?**
A: Monthly. Credited to your account on fixed date.

**Q: What's the minimum balance to earn interest?**
A: BDT 50,001. Below this, no interest is earned.

**Q: How much life insurance do I get?**
A: Depends on your average monthly balance (BDT 100K to 300K).

**Q: Who gets the insurance money if I die?**
A: Your nominee or beneficiary as per your account nomination.

**Q: Can I use the locker discount immediately?**
A: Yes, after account opening. Book a locker and enjoy 50% discount.

**Q: How do I claim the loan processing fee waiver?**
A: Just apply for a loan through Prime Bank. Waiver is automatic.

**Q: Is the insurance coverage free?**
A: Yes, completely free (complimentary).

**Q: Can I withdraw anytime?**
A: Yes, unlimited withdrawals anytime during bank hours or via ATM.
