## How to Apply?

### Required Documents

1. **Completed Account Opening Form**
   - Available at any Prime Bank branch

2. **Identity Proof Documents**
   - NID/Passport/Birth Certificate of:
     - Applicant
     - Nominee(s)

3. **Address Proof Document**
   - Recent copy of utility bill (Telephone/Electricity/Water bill, etc.)

4. **Income/Profession Proof Document**
   - Student Proof:
     - Valid Student ID, OR
     - Certification from School/College/University
     - Original document must be produced at time of account opening
   - Source of Fund:
     - Supporting document(s) to substantiate source of fund (parent/legal guardian if applicable)

5. **Other Documents**
   - Self-attested recent passport-size photo (2 copies) of applicant
   - 1 photo of nominee(s) attested by account holder
   - Proof of Submission of Tax Return (PSR) or E-TIN Certificate (where applicable)

### Opening Process

1. Visit nearest Prime Bank branch
2. Bring original student ID/certification and all required documents
3. Fill out the Account Opening Form
4. Submit documents for verification
5. Receive account confirmation and debit card within 24 hours

## FAQs

**Q: Do I need to show proof of employment?**
A: No, you only need to show valid student ID or certification from your school/college/university.

**Q: At what age do I need to close this account?**
A: You can continue using this account after turning 25. It will transition to Prime Savings Account features, but there's no forced closure.

**Q: Can I withdraw money anytime?**
A: Yes, you can withdraw anytime during bank working hours. The ATM debit card allows 24/7 access.

**Q: Is the insurance coverage automatic?**
A: Yes, insurance coverage is automatic with account opening. No additional premium required.

**Q: What happens if I need more than BDT 1 Lakh insurance coverage?**
A: You can opt for additional insurance products available at Prime Bank. Speak with your relationship manager.

**Q: Can I transfer funds internationally?**
A: Yes, through international remittance services. Contact your branch for details and fees.

**Q: How long does it take to open an account?**
A: Typically 24 hours for account opening and debit card delivery (may vary by branch).

**Q: Is there an interest rate lock?**
A: Interest rates are subject to change as per bank policy. You'll be notified in advance of any changes.
