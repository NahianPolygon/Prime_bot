## Account Benefits Explained

### Why BDT 100,000 Threshold for Interest?

**Banking Practice:**
- Most savings accounts require minimum balance for interest
- BDT 100,000 is the EOD (End-of-Day) threshold
- This protects bank's operational costs
- Industry standard in Bangladesh

**Strategy:**
- Keep balance above 100,000 to earn interest
- Even few thousand below = no interest that day
- Aim to maintain 120,000-150,000 buffer

### Why Different Interest Rates by Balance?

**Risk Management:**
- Larger deposits = more risk for bank
- Higher balance = higher investment potential
- Bank can invest larger amounts in secure instruments
- Interest passed on to depositor

**Customer Advantage:**
- Incentive to save more
- Higher savings = higher interest
- Encourages financial discipline
- Rewards loyal customers with larger balances

## FAQs

**Q: What's the minimum balance to earn interest?**
A: BDT 100,000 End-of-Day balance. Below this = no interest that day.

**Q: What if balance falls below 100,000 one day?**
A: You lose interest for that day only. When balance goes above 100,000 again, interest resumes.

**Q: When is interest paid?**
A: Monthly. Automatically credited to your account on a fixed date.

**Q: How much life insurance do I get?**
A: Depends on your average monthly balance. Ranges from BDT 100,000 to 500,000 maximum.

**Q: Is the insurance cost deducted from my account?**
A: No. Completely free and complimentary. No deductions.

**Q: How do I keep the debit card free after 1st year?**
A: Make 18 POS transactions (card swipes at merchants) per year. That's less than 2 per month.

**Q: What's POS transaction?**
A: When you swipe your card at a shop/online store to pay. ATM withdrawals don't count.

**Q: Can I have the debit card in two currencies?**
A: No, typically one card in primary currency (BDT). International use is built-in.

**Q: Can minors open this account alone?**
A: No. Minors need parent/legal guardian consent and operating mandate.

**Q: What if I need to withdraw large amounts?**
A: Unlimited withdrawals anytime. Inform bank in advance for large amounts for security.

**Q: How is insurance claim settled?**
A: Nominee contacts Prime Bank with death certificate. Settlement usually within 30 days.

**Q: Can I invest in Wage Earners' Bond with this account?**
A: Yes. This is the standard account for wage earner bond investments.

**Q: Is there an interest rate lock?**
A: No. Interest rates are subject to change per bank policy. You'll get advance notice.
