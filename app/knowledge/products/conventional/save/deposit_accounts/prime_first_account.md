## Overview

Prime First Account is specifically designed for young Bangladeshi students under 18 years old. It's managed by parents or legal guardians until the account holder reaches adulthood, providing a safe and secure introduction to banking.

---

## Eligibility

- **Age:** Under 18 years old
- **Nationality:** Resident Bangladeshi student
- **Guardian:** Account managed by parent or legal guardian until age 18
- **Citizenship:** Bangladeshi residents only

## How to Apply?

### Required Documents

1. **Completed Account Opening Form**
   - Available at any Prime Bank branch

2. **Identity Proof Documents** (copies)
   - NID/Passport/Birth Certificate of:
     - Parent/legal guardian
     - Student
     - Nominee(s)

3. **Address Proof Document**
   - Recent copy of utility bill (Telephone/Electricity/Water bill, etc.)

4. **Income/Profession Proof Document**
   - Student Proof:
     - Photocopy of School ID, OR
     - School Certificate, OR
     - Latest tuition fee payment slip
   - Guardian's Income Proof:
     - Supporting document(s) to substantiate source of fund

5. **Other Documents**
   - Recent passport-size photo (2 copies) of student
   - Recent passport-size photo (1 copy) of parent
   - Recent passport-size photo (1 copy) of nominee
   - Proof of Submission of Tax Return (PSR) or E-TIN Certificate of parent/legal guardian (where applicable)

### Opening Process

1. Visit nearest Prime Bank branch with required documents
2. Fill out the Account Opening Form
3. Submit all documents for verification
4. Guardian provides consent and signature
5. Receive account confirmation and debit card

## FAQs

**Q: At what age can my child open this account?**
A: Any student under 18 years old can open this account.

**Q: Who manages the account?**
A: The parent or legal guardian manages the account on behalf of the child until they turn 18.

**Q: What happens when the account holder turns 18?**
A: The account transitions to the individual's name with full operational control.

**Q: What if funds are needed urgently?**
A: Withdrawals can be made anytime during bank working hours. The ATM debit card allows 24/7 access.

**Q: Can the account be closed?**
A: Yes, both the account holder (with guardian consent) and guardian can request closure. The account automatically transfers to the individual at age 18.

**Q: Is there a maximum deposit limit?**
A: No maximum limit. Interest is paid on any amount.

**Q: How is interest calculated?**
A: Interest is calculated daily and paid half-yearly at 5% per annum.
