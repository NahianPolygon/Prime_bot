# Prime Porijon Savings Account

**Tagline:** Supporting Families Across Borders

Prime Porijon is a savings account designed for the families of Non-Resident Bangladeshi (NRB) remitters living in Bangladesh. It offers attractive interest, incentives, and life insurance coverage, helping Bangladeshi remitters living abroad support their family's financial well-being.

---

## Overview

Prime Porijon (Bengali word for "family") Savings Account is specifically designed for family members of Bangladeshi workers abroad. It rewards remittance recipients with attractive interest, complimentary life insurance on remitted amounts, and dual-currency debit card facilities—making it easy to manage family finances while supporting loved ones earning abroad.

---

## Eligibility

### For Account Holder (Beneficiary)

- **Age:** 18 to 65 years old
- **Nationality:** Resident Bangladeshi citizen
- **Relationship:** Family member of NRB remitter
- **Account Type:** Can be individual or joint

### Account Holder Operating Mandate

- **Sole Operator:** Beneficiary operates account alone
- **Joint Account Option:** Can open jointly with:
  - The NRB remitter, OR
  - Another Resident Bangladeshi
- **Key Rule:** Beneficiary must be the sole operator

### For Remitter (NRB)

- Working abroad legally
- Sending wage earner remittance
- Using Prime Exchange Co PTE Ltd. for transfers

## Insurance Coverage Details

### How Insurance Works

**Automatic Enrollment:**
- NRB remitter sends wage earner remittance through Prime Exchange Co PTE Ltd.
- Amount credited to beneficiary's Porijon account
- NRB automatically gets **30-day life insurance coverage**
- Coverage amount based on remittance tier

### Coverage Tiers

| Remittance Amount (BDT) | Insurance Coverage (BDT) | Coverage Period |
|------------------------|------------------------|-----------------|
| Up to 50,000 | 25,000 | 30 days |
| 50,001 - 100,000 | 75,000 | 30 days |
| More than 100,000 | 100,000 (max) | 30 days |

### Insurance Age Requirements

- **Minimum Age:** 18 years
- **Maximum Age at Enrollment:** 65 years
- **Coverage Termination Age:** 70 years
- **Health Requirement:** Must be in good health at enrollment

### Who Receives Insurance Money?

**Upon Death of Remitter:**
- Beneficiary of the Prime Porijon account can claim
- OR designated nominee can claim
- Family receives coverage amount as financial support

**Claim Process:**
- Contact Prime Bank branch or call insurance support line
- Provide death certificate and account documents
- Quick settlement process (usually within 30 days)

## FAQ - Wage Earner Remittance Insurance

### Q: What is wage earner remittance?

**A:** Salary or any legal income of a Bangladeshi living abroad, sent to Bangladesh through a legal banking channel. Examples:
- Monthly salary from Gulf countries
- Professional income from developed countries
- Legitimate overseas work income

### Q: Do I have to apply for the insurance?

**A:** No. It's automatic! When NRB sends remittance through Prime Exchange Co PTE Ltd., insurance is activated automatically.

### Q: Is there any cost or premium?

**A:** No. Completely free and complimentary. No premium deductions from remittance.

### Q: What if remittance is sent through other methods?

**A:** Insurance applies only if sent through Prime Exchange Co PTE Ltd. Regular Prime Bank transfers receive standard account benefits.

### Q: How long is the insurance valid?

**A:** 30 days from the date remittance is credited to the account.

### Q: What if NRB sends multiple remittances?

**A:** Each remittance activates 30-day coverage. So with monthly remittances, there's continuous coverage.

### Q: Can the beneficiary claim the insurance?

**A:** Only the remitter is insured. Upon remitter's death, the designated nominee or beneficiary receives the coverage amount.

### Q: What's the maximum coverage?

**A:** Maximum is BDT 100,000 per remittance. The tier is based on remittance amount (see coverage table).

### Q: How do I claim if something happens?

**A:** Contact your Prime Bank branch or call insurance support line. Provide necessary documents. Settlement is quick (30 days typically).

### Q: What if account holder is over 65?

**A:** Beneficiary can have account at any age. Insurance applies to NRB remitter aged 18-65. Coverage continues to age 70 if started before 65.
