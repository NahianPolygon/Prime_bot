## Interest Rate Breakdown

### Tiered Interest Structure

| Account Balance (BDT) | Interest Rate |
|----------------------|---------------|
| Below 100,000 | 3.00% |
| 100,001 - 10,00,000 | 3.50% |
| 10,00,001 - 50,00,000 | 4.00% |
| 50,00,001 and above | 4.50% |

### Interest Calculation
- Calculated on **daily balance**
- Paid **monthly**
- Automatic crediting to account
- Example: BDT 25,00,000 balance earns 4.00% = BDT 1,00,000/year

## How to Apply?

### Required Documents

1. **Completed Account Opening Form**
   - Available at any Prime Bank branch

2. **Identity Proof Document**
   - NID, OR
   - Passport, OR
   - Birth Certificate
   - Required for applicant(s) and nominee(s)

3. **Address Proof Document**
   - Recent copy of utility bill (Telephone/Electricity/Water bill, etc.)

4. **Income/Profession Proof Document** (Choose One)
   - **For Salaried Retirees:**
     - Pension statement, OR
     - Retirement certificate, OR
     - Bank statement showing pension credit
   
   - **For Still Employed:**
     - Salary Certificate, OR
     - Pay Slip, OR
     - Job ID
   
   - **For Business Owners:**
     - Trade license copy
   
   - **For Property Owners:**
     - Ownership documents, OR
     - Rent receipt, OR
     - Rental deed
   
   - **For Self-Employed:**
     - Professional certificate
   
   - **For Spouse Income:**
     - Spouse's income proof document

5. **Other Documents**
   - Self-attested recent passport-size photo (2 copies) of applicant(s)
   - 1 photo of nominee(s) attested by account holder
   - Proof of Submission of Tax Return (PSR) or E-TIN Certificate (where applicable)

### Opening Process

1. Visit nearest Prime Bank branch
2. Submit Account Opening Form and documents
3. Bank verifies age and income
4. Account opened same day
5. Receive debit card and banking information
6. Start earning interest immediately

## FAQs

**Q: Do I need to be 50 exactly, or can I be younger?**
A: Must be 50 years or above. No exceptions.

**Q: Can my spouse open a joint account?**
A: Yes. If spouse is also 50+, both can be joint account holders.

**Q: How does the interest work?**
A: Calculated daily, paid monthly. Higher balance = higher interest.

**Q: When do I get the monthly interest payment?**
A: Fixed date each month, automatically credited to your account.

**Q: What are the health benefits exactly?**
A: Discounted hospital services including consultation, treatment, diagnostics, and emergency care.

**Q: Which hospitals are in the network?**
A: Major private hospitals and medical centers nationwide. See hospital list or call branch for details.

**Q: Can my adult children be nominees?**
A: Yes. Nominee can be anyone you choose—children, spouse, or other family members.

**Q: Is there a minimum balance requirement?**
A: No. You can keep any amount, but you need BDT 100,001+ to earn full interest rates.

**Q: Can I withdraw anytime?**
A: Yes. Unlimited withdrawals anytime. ATM available 24/7.

**Q: How do I use the locker discount?**
A: Book a locker at your branch and show your 50 & Plus account. 50% discount applied automatically.

**Q: What if I need a loan?**
A: You can apply for personal loan, home loan, or other products at special rates for senior citizens.

**Q: Is my money safe?**
A: Yes. FDIC insured up to maximum limit per Bangladesh Bank guidelines.
