## Overview

Prime RFCD (Resident Foreign Currency Deposit) Account is designed for Bangladeshi citizens who frequently travel abroad or receive foreign currency remittances. It allows you to maintain multiple currency deposits (USD, GBP, Euro) safely while being able to withdraw in Bangladeshi Taka for local use or remit internationally anytime.

## What is RFCD Account?

RFCD stands for **Resident Foreign Currency Deposit Account**. It's a specialized account that allows resident Bangladeshis to:
- Deposit foreign currency earned from abroad
- Maintain multi-currency accounts
- Withdraw in local currency (BDT) for Bangladesh use
- Remit balance internationally anytime
- Enjoy complete flexibility in currency management

## How to Apply?

### Eligibility Requirements

- You are a Bangladeshi resident
- You are 18 years or older
- You have a valid passport with immigration stamp

### Required Documents

1. **Completed Account Opening Form**
   - Available at any Prime Bank branch

2. **Identity Proof Document**
   - Copy of Bangladeshi National ID (NID), OR
   - Bangladeshi Passport (copy)

3. **Address Proof Document**
   - Recent copy of utility bill (Telephone/Electricity/Water bill, etc.)
   - Or any proof of residence in Bangladesh

4. **Passport Documentation**
   - Copy of valid Bangladeshi passport
   - **IMPORTANT:** Passport must show immigration arrival stamp from abroad
   - Clearly visible date of return and entry stamp required

5. **Foreign Currency Declaration** (if applicable)
   - Written declaration from depositor mentioning:
     - Date of return from abroad
     - Total amount of foreign exchange brought in
     - Proof of legal earnings (salary slip, business documents, etc.)

6. **FMJ Form** (if deposit exceeds USD 10,000)
   - **FMJ = Foreign Money and Jewelry Declaration Form**
   - Required for deposits greater than USD 10,000
   - Declaration to customs authority
   - Shows legal source of foreign currency
   - Ensures compliance with Bangladesh Bank regulations

7. **Other Documents**
   - Self-attested recent passport-size photo (2 copies)
   - Nominee details and photo (1 copy attested by account holder)

### Opening Process

1. Visit any Prime Bank branch
2. Submit Account Opening Form
3. Provide all required documents including passport with immigration stamp
4. Bank verifies documents and eligibility
5. Make initial deposit (minimum USD 250 or equivalent)
6. Account activation
7. Receive account number and bank statements
8. Ready to receive and manage foreign currency

## Foreign Currency Declaration (FMJ Form)

### What is FMJ?
- **FMJ = Foreign Money and Jewelry Declaration**
- Required for amounts over USD 10,000 equivalent
- Submitted to customs authority
- Certifies legal source of currency

### Who Needs to File?
- Anyone depositing more than USD 10,000 equivalent
- Anyone bringing jewelry/precious metals
- Travelers with high-value foreign currency
- Business persons with significant earnings

### Information Required
- Detailed description of currency
- Total amount in each denomination
- Date of acquisition
- Source of currency
- Purpose of bringing to Bangladesh

### Processing
- Filed at airport/customs on arrival
- Bank assists with filing
- No fee for filing
- Protection against legal issues

## FAQs

**Q: Do I need to bring currency every time I return to Bangladesh?**
A: No. You can deposit whenever you have foreign currency. It's optional and flexible.

**Q: What if I want to withdraw more than USD 10,000?**
A: You can withdraw any amount. Just inform the bank in advance for processing.

**Q: Can I deposit foreign currency earned from online work?**
A: Yes. Valid earned foreign currency can be deposited (with declaration if over USD 10,000).

**Q: How often can I make deposits?**
A: Unlimited. Deposit every time you return from abroad or receive foreign currency.

**Q: What's the exchange rate for deposits?**
A: Bank's interbank exchange rate on the day of deposit. Competitive and fair rates.

**Q: Can I have multiple RFCD accounts?**
A: Yes, you can have separate accounts in different currencies if needed.

**Q: What if I don't use the account for a long time?**
A: No problem. There's no minimum balance or maintenance fee. Account remains active.

**Q: Can I send money to my child studying abroad?**
A: Yes. Remit to international accounts for education expenses, living costs, etc.

**Q: Is the account insured?**
A: Yes, FDIC insured up to maximum limit per Bangladesh Bank guidelines.

**Q: How long does account opening take?**
A: Typically 2-3 business days from document submission.

**Q: What's the minimum initial deposit?**
A: USD 250 or equivalent in GBP/Euro.

**Q: Can I convert between currencies in my account?**
A: Yes, convert between USD/GBP/Euro anytime at bank rates.

**Q: What currencies are supported?**
A: Currently USD (Dollar), GBP (Pound Sterling), and EUR (Euro).
