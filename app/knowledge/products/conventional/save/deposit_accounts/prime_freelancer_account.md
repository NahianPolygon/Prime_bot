**Tagline:** Optimized My Income, Become My Own Boss

Experience personalized financial solutions designed for your unique needs as a Freelancer.

## Overview

Prime Freelancer Account is a **bundled solution specifically designed for Bangladeshi freelancers earning in foreign currency**. It combines a USD Foreign Currency (FCY) Account with a BDT Savings Account, enabling you to retain up to 35% of your freelance income in USD while managing your finances seamlessly.

---

## What is Prime Freelancer Account Bundle?

Prime Freelancer Account is an **Exporters' Retention Quota (ERQ) Account** for individual freelancers. It includes:

1. **Prime Freelancer ERQ Account** (USD)
   - Foreign Currency Account for USD retention
   
2. **Prime One Savings Account** (BDT) - Linked
   - Bangladeshi Taka account for remaining balance
   - Completes your bundled solution

---

## Eligibility

- **Nationality:** Resident Bangladeshi Freelancer
- **Age:** 18 years old or above
- **Income Source:** Earning Foreign Currency (FCY) through outsourcing from abroad
- **Banking Requirement:** Must have a Prime One Savings Account (or can open simultaneously)
- **Account Type:** Individual only (joint accounts not allowed)

---

## What is the ERQ Account?

### Exporters' Retention Quota (ERQ)
- Exporters are permitted to retain a portion of their export earnings in foreign currency (FCY)
- Freelancers are considered exporters since they earn FCY through outsourcing services
- This account maintains your retained FCY earnings
- **Current ERQ Limit:** 35% of inward remittance (65% goes to linked BDT account)

## How to Apply?

### Required Documents

1. **Completed Account Opening Form**
   - Available at any Prime Bank AD Branch

2. **Identity Proof Document**
   - NID, OR
   - Passport, OR
   - Birth Certificate
   - Required for applicant and nominee(s)

3. **Address Proof Document**
   - Recent copy of utility bill (Telephone/Electricity/Water bill, etc.)

4. **Income/Profession Proof Document** (Choose one)
   - **Government Authority Option:**
     - ID/Testimonial/Certificate issued by government authority
   
   - **Professional Organization Option:**
     - Bangladesh Association of Software and Information Services (BASIS) Certificate
     - Bangladesh Freelancer Development Society (BFDS) Certificate or member organization certificate
   
   - **Freelance Platform Option:**
     - Freelancer's profile screenshot from marketplace (Upwork, Fiverr, etc.)
     - Valid work order from client
     - Email/communication containing work order of last 3 items worked on (mentioning amount earned in FCY)

   **Note:** Additional documents may be required for specific services like business services, professional/research and advising services.

5. **Other Documents**
   - Self-attested recent passport-size photo (2 copies) of applicant
   - 1 photo of nominee(s) attested by account holder
   - Proof of Submission of Tax Return (PSR) or E-TIN Certificate (where applicable)

### Opening Process

1. Visit any Prime Bank **AD (Authorized Dealer) Branch**
2. Submit completed application with all documents
3. Bank verifies freelance credentials and income source
4. Open linked Prime One Savings Account (if not existing)
5. Receive USD account confirmation and International Debit Card
6. Start receiving freelance payments in USD

## ERQ Limit Explained

### How ERQ Works

**Example:** Your freelance income = USD 1,000

| Component | Amount | Destination |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| **Total Income** | USD 1,000 | - |
| **ERQ Limit (35%)** | USD 350 | Prime Freelancer ERQ Account |
| **Mandatory Credit (65%)** | USD 650 | Linked Prime One Savings Account (BDT equivalent) |

### ERQ Limit Changes
- Current limit: 35% (as per Bangladesh Bank guideline)
- Subject to change: ERQ quota may be changed as per Bangladesh Bank directive

## FAQs

**Q: Can I have multiple Freelancer ERQ Accounts?**
A: No. You can only have one Prime Freelancer ERQ Account. Joint accounts are not allowed.

**Q: Can Foreigners or Non-Resident Bangladeshis open this account?**
A: No. Only Resident Bangladeshi citizens can open this account.

**Q: What happens if I earn more than the 35% ERQ limit?**
A: You can retain 35% in USD. The remaining 65% is automatically credited to your linked BDT account, which you can use or convert to USD later.

**Q: Can the International Debit Card be used in Bangladesh?**
A: Currently, the International Debit Card cannot be used in local ATM/POS inside Bangladesh. It can only be used abroad.

**Q: What if the ERQ quota changes?**
A: If Bangladesh Bank changes the ERQ limit, you'll be notified in advance. Your existing balance won't be affected, but new deposits will follow the new limit.

**Q: Do I need to have a Prime One account already?**
A: No. You can open both accounts simultaneously. They'll be linked automatically.

**Q: How long does account opening take?**
A: Typically 3-5 business days from document submission.

**Q: What if my freelance income is in currencies other than USD?**
A: The account is USD-only. Other currencies must be converted to USD first before depositing.

**Q: Is there a maximum deposit limit?**
A: No. You can deposit any amount up to your ERQ limit (35% of remittance).

**Q: How do I transfer funds to the BDT account?**
A: Your 65% share is transferred automatically. For additional USD-to-BDT conversions, visit any AD Branch.
