## How to Apply?

### Required Documents

1. **Completed Account Opening Form**
   - Available at any Prime Bank branch

2. **Identity Proof Document**
   - NID, OR
   - Passport, OR
   - Birth Certificate
   - Required for applicant(s) and nominee(s)

3. **Address Proof Document**
   - Recent copy of utility bill (Telephone/Electricity/Water bill, etc.)

4. **Income/Profession Proof Document** (Choose One)
   - **NO trade license required**
   - Instead, provide an **attestation or certificate** from:
     - **Local Public Representative** (Union Councilor, Upazila Chairman representative, etc.), OR
     - **Professional Association** relevant to your profession
   - Certificate must confirm:
     - Your profession/business type
     - Duration of operation
     - Legitimacy of income source

5. **Other Documents**
   - Self-attested recent passport-size photo (2 copies) of applicant(s)
   - 1 photo of nominee(s) attested by account holder
   - Proof of Submission of Tax Return (PSR) or E-TIN Certificate (where applicable)
   - FATCA (Foreign Account Tax Compliance Act) form

### Opening Process

1. Visit nearest Prime Bank branch
2. Explain your profession/business to bank officer
3. Bank guides you on getting local public representative or professional association certificate
4. Submit completed form with certificate (not trade license)
5. Account activation with zero balance
6. Start using immediately

## Income Verification Without Trade License

### Why the Alternative Approach?

Many small business owners don't have formal trade licenses because:
- Too expensive to register
- Too much bureaucracy
- Not required for informal businesses
- Government encourages financial inclusion

### How to Get Attestation

**Option 1: Local Public Representative**
1. Visit your Union Councilor or Upazila representative
2. Explain your business
3. Request a simple letter/certificate confirming:
   - Your profession
   - Duration of operation
   - That you're doing legitimate work
4. Get the certificate signed and stamped

**Option 2: Professional Association**
1. Join relevant professional association (if available):
   - Mechanics Association
   - Electricians Guild
   - Traders Association
   - Service Providers Union
2. Get membership certificate
3. Association provides income confirmation

## FAQs

**Q: Do I need a trade license?**
A: No. Just get a certificate from your local public representative or professional association.

**Q: What if I don't have a certificate yet?**
A: Visit your Union Councilor first—they usually provide it free or at minimal cost. It takes 1-2 days.

**Q: Can I open with zero balance?**
A: Yes. Completely. Start saving when you can.

**Q: Can I use this for personal and business?**
A: Yes. It's designed for both purposes.

**Q: Are there any hidden fees?**
A: No. Completely transparent and free.

**Q: How long does it take to open?**
A: Usually same day if documents are complete.

**Q: Can I get a cheque book?**
A: Yes, first cheque book is free.

**Q: Can I withdraw anytime?**
A: Yes, unlimited withdrawals anytime.

**Q: Is my money safe?**
A: Yes, FDIC insured up to maximum limit per Bangladesh Bank guidelines.

**Q: Can someone else operate this account?**
A: No, only account holder can operate. For business partners, open joint account.
