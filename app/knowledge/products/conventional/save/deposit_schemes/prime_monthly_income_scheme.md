# Prime Monthly Income Scheme (MIS)

**Tagline:** Invest & Earn Extra

Prime Monthly Income Scheme (MIS) is an investment-nature single installment deposit scheme where customers earn a fixed monthly income against their principal deposit amount, making it ideal for retirees and those seeking regular income.

---

## Overview

Prime Monthly Income Scheme (MIS) is a unique deposit scheme designed for those who want regular monthly income without touching their principal. With deposits starting from BDT 1 lakh in multiples, you receive guaranteed monthly income (interest) while your principal remains safe and untouched.

---

## What is MIS?

**MIS** provides:
- **Stable monthly income** regardless of market conditions
- **Principal protection** - your money stays safe
- **Guaranteed returns** - fixed monthly payout
- **Auto-renewal** of principal at maturity
- **Overdraft facility** for emergencies

Perfect for retirees, investors seeking regular income, and those wanting predictable cash flow.

---

## Eligibility

- **Age:** Any Bangladeshi citizen of any age

- **Account Requirement:** Active linked/transactional current or savings account with Prime Bank
- **Minor Accounts:** Can be opened singly or jointly with parent/legal guardian
- **Minimum Deposit:** BDT 100,000
- **Deposit Multiples:** Any amount in multiples of BDT 100,000

## How to Apply?

### Required Documents

1. **Completed Personal Savings Scheme Account Opening Form**
   - Available at Prime Bank branch
   - All fields filled clearly

2. **Active Linked Account**
   - Current or Savings account with Prime Bank
   - Account must be active (transactional)
   - Opened BEFORE MIS opening

3. **Identification**
   - NID, Passport, or Birth Certificate
   - For account holder

4. **Income/Profession Proof** (optional)
   - For verification purposes
   - Source of deposit fund

5. **Nominee Details**
   - Nominee name and relationship
   - Nominee identity proof

### Opening Process

1. Visit Prime Bank branch with lump sum deposit (multiple of 1 lac)
2. Request MIS Account Opening Form
3. Choose tenor: 1-year, 3-year, or 5-year
4. Complete application form
5. Specify nominee details
6. Submit documents and deposit amount
7. Scheme activated
8. Monthly income transfers start

## FAQs

**Q: When do I receive my monthly income?**
A: Transferred to your linked account on fixed date each month.

**Q: Can I change tenor mid-way?**
A: No. Tenor is fixed at opening. Close and reopen for different tenor.

**Q: What happens to principal?**
A: Fully returned at maturity. Not touched during tenure.

**Q: Can I borrow against MIS?**
A: Yes. Overdraft facility available up to MIS amount.

**Q: What if I need my money before maturity?**
A: You can close, but interest may be reduced. Contact bank for early closure terms.

**Q: Can I open multiple MIS?**
A: Yes. One person can have multiple MIS with different tenors and amounts.

**Q: Is monthly income guaranteed?**
A: Yes. Fixed monthly amount guaranteed regardless of market conditions.

**Q: How long does it take to activate?**
A: Usually same day or next business day.

**Q: Is tax deducted from monthly income?**
A: Yes. Tax may be applicable as per income tax laws.

**Q: Can minors open MIS?**
A: Yes. With parent/legal guardian and operating mandate.
