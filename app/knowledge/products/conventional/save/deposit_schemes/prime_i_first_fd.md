# Prime I-First FD

**Tagline:** Start with a Win - Earn Interest on Day One

Prime I-First FD is a unique fixed deposit account that lets you earn interest on the first day. The principal amount is returned upon maturity, making it a winning investment from day one.

## Overview

Prime I-First FD is a unique fixed deposit scheme offering advance interest paid on the opening day itself, with principal returned at maturity. With fixed 12-month tenure and a minimum deposit of BDT 1 lakh, this scheme provides immediate liquidity while locking in long-term wealth accumulation.

---

## What Makes I-First FD Unique?

**I-First** means:
- **Interest Paid Upfront** on the first day
- **Principal Protected** until maturity
- **No Waiting** for returns (cash in hand immediately)
- **Secure Investment** with bank guarantee

Perfect for those who want immediate cash benefit from their deposit while keeping principal safe for 12 months.

## How to Apply?

### Required Documents

1. **Passport Size Photograph**
   - Depositor attested (1 copy)
   - Nominee attested (1 copy)

2. **Identity Documents**
   - NID, Passport, or Birth Certificate
   - For depositor and nominee

3. **Minor Account Formalities** (if applicable)
   - Parent/legal guardian consent
   - Operating mandate documentation

4. **Linked Account Requirement**
   - Active current or savings account
   - Must be with Prime Bank
   - Opened BEFORE I-First FD opening
   - Will receive interest and principal

5. **Application Form**
   - Complete Personal FD Account Opening Form
   - Specify deposit amount (minimum 1 lakh)
   - Nominee information provided

### Opening Process

1. Visit Prime Bank branch with BDT 1+ lakh
2. Request "I-First FD" opening form
3. Complete application with nominee details
4. Deposit your lump sum amount
5. Submit all documents
6. **Same day:** Interest amount credited to linked account
7. Receive FD receipt with maturity date and principal amount
8. After 12 months: Principal automatically returned

## Pre-Maturity Encashment

### Early Withdrawal Rules

**If account is closed before 1 month:**
- No interest paid
- Principal only returned
- Interest adjustment policy

**If account is closed after 1 month but before maturity:**
- Interest at Prime Savings Account rate (~2-4%)
- Not full FD rate
- Significantly reduced than I-First benefit

**Important:** Upfront interest ALREADY PAID cannot be recovered. Only principal subject to these rules.

## FAQs

**Q: When exactly is interest paid?**
A: Immediately on FD opening (same day, possibly same hour).

**Q: Can I get more interest later?**
A: No. All interest paid upfront. At maturity, only principal returned.

**Q: What if I close before 12 months?**
A: Upfront interest already paid (yours to keep). Principal withdrawal subject to forfeiture rules.

**Q: Can I extend after 12 months?**
A: Yes. At maturity, open new I-First FD or regular FD.

**Q: Is the interest rate guaranteed?**
A: Yes. Fixed for your 12-month tenure.

**Q: Can I open multiple I-First FDs?**
A: Yes. As many as you want, amounts you choose (minimum 1 lakh each).

**Q: What if I need principal before 12 months?**
A: You can close early, but interest penalty applies (you lose portion of interest benefit). Upfront interest already paid is yours.

**Q: Can I increase amount after opening?**
A: No. Separate FD needed for additional amount.

**Q: Is tax deducted from interest?**
A: Yes. Tax on interest as per income tax laws (typically ~10-12%).

**Q: Why is this unique compared to regular FD?**
A: Most FDs pay interest at maturity. I-First FD pays upfront (Day 1), giving you immediate cash benefit while keeping principal locked.
