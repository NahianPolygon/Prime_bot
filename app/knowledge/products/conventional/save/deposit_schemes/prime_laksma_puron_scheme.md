## Overview

Prime Laksma Puron Scheme is a flexible monthly installment deposit scheme that lets you choose your target amount and tenure. With attractive 6% interest, automatic monthly deposits, and loan facility, Laksma Puron helps you systematically achieve your custom financial goals—whatever the amount.

---

## What is Laksma Puron?

**Laksma Puron** means "filling your coffers" in Bengali. This scheme lets you:
- **Set your own target amount** (any amount you choose)
- **Choose flexible tenure** (3, 4, or 5 years)
- **Fixed monthly deposit** (calculated based on your goal)
- **Achieve your custom goal** systematically through discipline

## How to Apply?

### Required Documents

1. **Passport Size Photograph**
   - Depositor attested (1 copy)
   - Nominee attested (1 copy)

2. **Identity Documents**
   - NID, Passport, or Birth Certificate
   - For depositor and nominee

3. **Minor Account Formalities** (if applicable)
   - Parent/legal guardian consent
   - Operating mandate documentation

4. **Linked Account Requirement**
   - Active current or savings account
   - Must have sufficient balance for monthly deduction
   - Account with Prime Bank

5. **Application Form**
   - Complete Laksma Puron Scheme opening form
   - Specify target goal amount
   - Select tenure (3/4/5 years)
   - Nominee information filled

### Opening Process

1. Visit Prime Bank branch
2. Request Laksma Puron Scheme form
3. Specify your goal amount
4. Choose tenure (3, 4, or 5 years)
5. Bank calculates monthly installment
6. Fill application and nominee details
7. Submit documents
8. Set up automatic monthly deduction
9. Receive scheme documentation

## Pre-Maturity Encashment

### Early Withdrawal Rules

**Before 12 months:**
- No interest paid
- Principal only returned

**12-60 months:**
- Interest at Prime Savings Account rate
- Not full scheme rate

**60+ months:**
- Scheme rate for 60 months
- Remaining period at Prime Savings rate

## FAQs

**Q: Can I choose any amount as my goal?**
A: Yes. Any amount you decide (see calculation tables for examples).

**Q: Is 6% rate guaranteed for all tenors?**
A: Yes. 6% fixed across 3, 4, and 5-year plans.

**Q: How is monthly amount calculated?**
A: Based on your goal, tenure, and 6% interest rate.

**Q: Can I change monthly amount mid-way?**
A: No. Amount is fixed at opening. Open new scheme for different amount.

**Q: What if I miss a monthly payment?**
A: Scheme may be suspended. Contact bank immediately to reschedule.

**Q: Can I borrow against this scheme?**
A: Yes. Loan facility available up to scheme value.

**Q: Can I open multiple Laksma Puron schemes?**
A: Yes. One person can have multiple schemes with different goals.

**Q: How long does it take to activate?**
A: Usually same day or next business day.

**Q: Is tax deducted from interest?**
A: Yes. Tax applicable as per income tax laws.

**Q: What happens if I need money early?**
A: Early encashment available with interest penalty.
