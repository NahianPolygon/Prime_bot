# Prime Edu DPS

**Tagline:** Invest for Education, Secure the Future

Prime Edu DPS is a specialized education-focused monthly installment deposit scheme designed to build the funds needed for your child's higher education through systematic, disciplined savings.

---

## Overview

Prime Edu DPS is a dedicated educational savings scheme where parents/guardians systematically save for their child's education through monthly deposits. With excellent maturity values and flexible long tenors (5/10/15/20 years), this scheme ensures your child's education is never compromised by financial constraints.

---

## What is Edu DPS?

**Edu DPS** provides:
- **Education-focused savings** for child's future
- **Long tenors** (5-20 years) matching education timeline
- **Flexible monthly installments** from BDT 500
- **Excellent maturity values** (see tables)
- **9% fixed interest** across all tenors

---

## Eligibility

- **Account Holder:** Bangladeshi citizen of any age

- **Minor Accounts:** Can be opened for minor child with parent/guardian operating mandate

## How to Apply?

### Required Documents

1. **Completed Personal Savings Scheme Account Opening Form**
   - Filled with all details
   - Educational goal specified

2. **Active Linked CASA Account**
   - Current or Savings account
   - With Prime Bank
   - Opened BEFORE Edu DPS

3. **Identity Documents**
   - Account holder ID proof
   - Parent/guardian documents (if minor)
   - Child's birth certificate or ID

4. **Beneficiary Information**
   - Child's name and age
   - Expected education level
   - Estimated expenses (optional)

### Opening Process

1. Visit Prime Bank branch
2. Request "Prime Edu DPS" application form
3. Choose tenor (5/10/15/20 years) based on child's age
4. Decide monthly installment amount
5. Complete application with child details
6. Provide all documents
7. Set up automatic monthly deduction
8. Receive Edu DPS documentation
9. Track progress online

## Pre-Maturity Encashment

### Early Withdrawal Rules

**If encashed before 12 months:**
- No interest paid
- Principal only

**If encashed before 60 months:**
- Interest at Prime Savings Account rate (~2-4%)
- Reduced from 9% scheme rate

**If encashed before 120 months:**
- First 60 months at Scheme Rate (9%)
- Remaining months at Prime Savings Rate

**If encashed before 180 months:**
- First 120 months at Scheme Rate
- Remaining months at Prime Savings Rate

**If encashed before 240 months:**
- First 180 months at Scheme Rate
- Remaining months at Prime Savings Rate

## FAQs

**Q: Can I open Edu DPS if my child is not born yet?**
A: Yes. Open in your own name with intention to transfer to child's education later.

**Q: What if I skip a month?**
A: Payment irregularity reduces final maturity amount. Maintain consistency.

**Q: Can I change monthly amount?**
A: No. Amount is fixed. Open additional scheme for more savings.

**Q: When should I open this scheme?**
A: Earlier the better! From birth onwards, more time = more compound interest.

**Q: Can I transfer to my child?**
A: Discuss with bank regarding guardianship transfer and account modification.

**Q: What if my child doesn't need full amount at maturity?**
A: Withdraw what's needed. Remaining can stay in account or be reinvested.

**Q: Is tax deducted?**
A: Yes. Tax on interest as per income tax laws (~10-12% typical).

**Q: Can multiple people open for same child?**
A: Yes. Parents, grandparents, others can each open separate Edu DPS for child.

**Q: What tenor for a 10-year-old?**
A: 5-year plan (mature at age 15-16, before college) or loan for immediate education needs.

**Q: Can I borrow for the child's education now?**
A: Yes. Emergency loan up to 90% of deposited amount available anytime.
