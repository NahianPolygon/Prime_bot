**Tagline:** Toward Big Changes

## Overview

Prime Lakhopoti Deposit Scheme is a monthly installment deposit scheme designed to build wealth of BDT 1 lakh or more through systematic savings. With flexible tenors (3, 5, 10, 15 years) and terminal benefit payouts, Lakhopoti helps you achieve your first major financial milestone in an achievable timeframe.

---

## What is Lakhopoti?

**Lakhopoti** represents the first significant wealth milestone - accumulating one lakh (BDT 100,000) and beyond. Prime Lakhopoti Scheme guarantees:
- **Terminal Benefit:** BDT 1 lakh guaranteed at maturity
- **Plus Accumulated Interest:** Additional returns on your deposits
- **Flexible Timeline:** 3, 5, 10, or 15-year options
- **Automatic Realization:** Monthly installments processed automatically

## Lakhopoti Advantage: Terminal Benefit Explained

### What is Terminal Benefit?

The **Terminal Benefit** is a guaranteed minimum payout of **BDT 1 lac** at the end of your scheme tenure, PLUS you get accumulated interest on all your deposits.

### Example: 5-Year Lakhopoti @ 6%

**Your Investment:**

- Total deposit: BDT 87,000

## How to Apply?

### Required Documents

1. **Passport Size Photograph**
   - Depositor duly attested by depositor (1 photo)
   - Nominee duly attested by depositor (1 photo)
   - Recent, clear photographs

2. **Identity Documents**
   - NID, OR Passport, OR Birth Registration Certificate
   - For both depositor and nominee

3. **Minor Account Formalities**
   - Parent/legal guardian consent
   - Operating mandate documentation
   - Guardian's identity proof

4. **Linked Account Requirement**
   - Must have active transaction/savings account
   - Account opened BEFORE scheme opening
   - Account must be with Prime Bank

5. **Application Form**
   - Complete Personal Savings Scheme opening form
   - Sign and date all fields
   - Declare nominee information

### Opening Steps

1. Visit Prime Bank branch
2. Request Lakhopoti Deposit Scheme form
3. Choose tenor (3/5/10/15 years)
4. Fill out application with nominee details
5. Submit documents and form
6. Activate automatic monthly deduction
7. Receive scheme documentation

## Pre-Maturity Encashment

### Early Withdrawal Rules

**Closed before 12 months:**
- No interest paid
- Receive principal only
- Terminal benefit forfeited

**Closed 12-60 months:**
- Interest at Prime Savings rate
- Terminal benefit still applies
- Interest calculated for period held

**Closed 60+ months:**
- Scheme-rate interest for 60 months
- Remaining period at Prime Savings rate
- Terminal benefit applies

## FAQs

**Q: Can I start a Lakhopoti scheme at any age?**
A: Yes, 18 years and above. No upper age limit for opening.

**Q: What is the guaranteed terminal benefit?**
A: BDT 100,000. This is guaranteed regardless of interest rates.

**Q: Can I open multiple Lakhopoti schemes?**
A: Yes. One person can open multiple schemes with different tenors.

**Q: What if I stop payments?**
A: Scheme may be cancelled. Contact bank immediately to resolve.

**Q: Can I borrow before maturity?**
A: Yes. Loan facility available against your deposits.

**Q: What happens if I need money before maturity?**
A: You can close scheme early, but with interest penalty. Terminal benefit reduced.

**Q: Are interest rates fixed?**
A: Yes. Rates locked for your entire scheme tenure.

**Q: How long does it take to reach BDT 1 lac?**
A: As fast as 3 years with highest monthly amount, or as low as BDT 320/month for 15 years.

**Q: Is tax deducted from interest?**
A: Yes. Tax applicable per income tax laws.

**Q: Can a joint account holder open this?**
A: Individual accounts only. One name per scheme.
