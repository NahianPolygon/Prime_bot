# Prime Deposit Premium Scheme

**Tagline:** Premium Savings with Premium Returns

Prime Deposit Premium Scheme is a monthly installment/recurring deposit scheme where customers make monthly deposits of equal amount and receive substantial lump sum at maturity.

---

## Overview

Prime Deposit Premium Scheme is a disciplined monthly deposit scheme with impressive maturity values. With installments from BDT 500 and flexible tenors (3/5/7/10 years), this scheme delivers excellent returns through consistent monthly savings—perfect for those wanting to build wealth systematically with high interest (9%).

## Deposit Premium Calculation Formula

For installments OTHER than those in the table above:

**Formula:** (Your Installment ÷ 500) × Maturity Value for 500

### Example Calculation

**Your Plan:**
- Tenure: 5 years
- Monthly: BDT 2,500

**Calculation:**
- Divisor: BDT 2,500 ÷ BDT 500 = 5
- Base maturity (500 @ 5yr): BDT 37,925
- Your maturity: BDT 37,925 × 5 = **BDT 189,625**

## Pre-Maturity Encashment

### Early Withdrawal Rules

**If encashed before 12 months:**
- No interest paid
- Principal only returned

**If encashed before 36 months:**
- Interest at Prime Savings Account rate (~2-4%)
- Not full 9% scheme rate

**If encashed before 60 months:**
- First 36 months at Scheme Rate (9%)
- Remaining months at Prime Savings Rate
- Blended interest applies

**If encashed before 84 months:**
- First 60 months at Scheme Rate
- Remaining months at Prime Savings Rate

**If encashed before 120 months:**
- First 84 months at Scheme Rate
- Remaining months at Prime Savings Rate

## FAQs

**Q: Can I change monthly amount?**
A: No. Amount is fixed at opening. Open another scheme for different amount.

**Q: What happens if I miss a month?**
A: Payment irregularity reduces final maturity amount. Maintain consistency.

**Q: Is 9% rate guaranteed?**
A: Yes. Fixed for entire tenure regardless of market conditions.

**Q: Can I open multiple schemes?**
A: Yes. One person can have multiple Deposit Premium schemes.

**Q: When do I receive maturity amount?**
A: Lump sum at end of tenure. Not monthly.

**Q: Can I borrow during the scheme?**
A: Yes. Loan up to 90% of deposits available anytime.

**Q: What if maturity value shown is before tax?**
A: Plan for ~10-12% tax on interest earned. Net amount will be less.

**Q: Can minors open this scheme?**
A: Yes. With parent/guardian operating mandate.

**Q: How is maturity amount calculated for non-standard amounts?**
A: Use formula: (Your amount ÷ 500) × Table value for 500.

**Q: Is automatic deduction mandatory?**
A: Yes. Monthly installment automatically deducted from linked account.
