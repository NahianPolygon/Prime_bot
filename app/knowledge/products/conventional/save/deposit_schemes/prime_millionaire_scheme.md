## Overview

Prime Millionaire Scheme is a fixed-monthly-installment deposit scheme designed specifically to help you achieve your first million. With fixed, affordable monthly installments and attractive interest rates (6-7%), this scheme makes wealth accumulation simple and achievable for anyone earning a regular income.

## How to Apply?

### Required Documents

1. **Passport Size Photograph**
   - Of depositor duly attested by depositor
   - Of nominee duly attested by depositor
   - Recent, clear photos (2 of each)

2. **Identity Documents**
   - NID (National ID), OR
   - Passport, OR
   - Birth Registration Certificate
   - Required for both depositor and nominee

3. **Minor Account Formalities**
   - If depositor is minor:
     - Parent/legal guardian's consent
     - Operating mandate documentation
     - Guardian's identity proof

4. **Linked Account Requirement**
   - Active transaction/savings account with Prime Bank
   - Account must be opened BEFORE scheme opening
   - Should have sufficient balance for monthly deductions

### Opening Process

1. Visit Prime Bank branch with documents
2. Request "Prime Millionaire Scheme" opening form
3. Fill out scheme application with tenor preference
4. Submit documents and form
5. Select monthly installment amount
6. Activate automatic deduction from linked account
7. Get scheme reference number and documentation

## Loan Facility Against Millionaire Scheme

### Loan Availability

- **Maximum:** 90% of total deposited amount
- **Interest:** Bank's prevailing loan rate
- **Tenure:** Flexible, up to original scheme tenure
- **Purpose:** Any personal or business use

### How Loan Works

1. Request loan at any time during scheme
2. Bank calculates 90% of deposits made so far
3. Loan approved and disbursed within 2-3 days
4. Continue monthly installments as usual
5. Loan repayment term flexible
6. At maturity: Loan adjusted, remaining balance credited

### Example: Loan Against Millionaire Scheme

**Profile:**
- Tenor: 7-year Millionaire Scheme
- Monthly: BDT 9,800
- After 2 years: Total deposited BDT 2,35,200
- Need: Emergency fund

**Loan Available:**
- 90% of BDT 2,35,200 = BDT 2,11,680
- Can borrow up to BDT 2,11,680
- Scheme continues running
- Monthly BDT 9,800 payments continue

## FAQs

**Q: What is the minimum age to open this scheme?**
A: 18 years old (must be adult, self-signed application).

**Q: Can I open multiple Millionaire Schemes?**
A: Yes. One person can open more than one scheme at different tenors.

**Q: What if I miss a monthly payment?**
A: Scheme may be suspended or cancelled. Contact bank immediately to reschedule.

**Q: Can I close the scheme early?**
A: Yes, but you may lose some interest. Early encashment rules apply (see pre-maturity section).

**Q: Can I change the monthly amount?**
A: No. Amount is fixed at opening. You can open additional schemes for more savings.

**Q: What if I can't pay one month?**
A: Contact bank to discuss options. Late payment affects final amount.

**Q: Are the interest rates guaranteed?**
A: Yes. Rates are fixed for entire scheme tenure.

**Q: How much will I have at maturity?**
A: Total deposited + interest earned. See maturity projection tables above.

**Q: Can a housewife open this scheme?**
A: Yes. Any Bangladeshi citizen 18+ can open, regardless of employment.

**Q: Is tax deducted from interest?**
A: Yes. Tax per income tax laws applicable to interest income.
