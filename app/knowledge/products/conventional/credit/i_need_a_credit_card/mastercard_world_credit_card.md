## FAQs

**Q: Why 2 points per BDT 50 when others give 1?**
A: Mastercard World premium positioning offers double earning to maximize cardholder rewards.

**Q: Can I use points for any redemption?**
A: Points are flexible. Use for flights, hotels, shopping, or special redemptions via 16218.

**Q: What if I have multiple Mastercard World cards?**
A: Points accumulate across all cards. Check account for combined balance.

**Q: Is annual fee always charged?**
A: Waived if you make 15+ purchases in a year. Easy to achieve with regular usage.

**Q: Can I add 3rd companion to lounge access?**
A: Balaka VIP includes you + 2 companions. LoungeKey additional visits possible with spend.

**Q: What's the minimum payment required?**
A: Minimum 5% of outstanding or BDT 5,000, whichever is higher.

**Q: Is travel insurance included?**
A: Yes, automatic travel insurance included. Covers medical, trip cancellation, luggage.

**Q: How do I claim airport welcome?**
A: Contact MGA 24 hours before arrival with flight details. They'll arrange.

**Q: Are double points better than single point cards?**
A: Yes! Double earning means BDT 48,000 annual value vs BDT 24,000 on single-point cards.

**Q: Can I extend the 50-day grace period?**
A: No, fixed at 50 days. Use 0% EMI for larger purchases requiring longer tenure.
