## FAQs

**Q: What's included in LoungeKey access?**
A: 1,400+ premium lounges globally. Fee waived if you spend USD 500 during travel.

**Q: Can I use BOGO at any restaurant?**
A: Only at 6 specified luxury partners: Westin, InterContinental, Le Meridien, Radisson Blu, Sheraton, Pan Pacific.

**Q: How does airport welcome work?**
A: Contact MGA (numbers/email provided) 24 hours before arrival. 2 guests assistance included.

**Q: Is travel insurance automatic?**
A: Yes, travel insurance included automatically. Covers medical emergencies, trip cancellation, luggage.

**Q: What if I exceed credit limit?**
A: Over-limit fee BDT 1,000 charged. Strongly advised to stay within limit.

**Q: Can I increase limit anytime?**
A: Yes, after 6 months with good payment history. Automatic annual review.

**Q: Is the 50-day grace period extendable?**
A: No, fixed at 50 days. Use 0% EMI for larger purchases instead.

**Q: How many EMI purchases can I have simultaneously?**
A: Multiple EMIs allowed as long as total is within credit limit.

**Q: What's the minimum payment?**
A: Minimum 5% of outstanding or BDT 5,000, whichever is higher.

**Q: Are reward points taxable?**
A: Generally non-taxable as marketing incentives. Consult tax advisor.
