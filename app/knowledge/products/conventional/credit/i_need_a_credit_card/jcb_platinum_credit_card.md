## FAQs

**Q: What's the minimum payment required?**
A: Minimum 5% of outstanding or BDT 5,000, whichever is higher.

**Q: How can I get the BOGO benefit?**
A: Visit partner restaurant with JCB Platinum, present card at billing, BOGO applied automatically.

**Q: Is annual fee always charged?**
A: No. Make 15+ purchases in a year = automatic waiver.

**Q: Can I use EMI multiple times?**
A: Yes. Multiple EMIs allowed as long as total is within credit limit.

**Q: What happens if I miss the 50-day deadline?**
A: Interest (1.5% monthly) accrues on unpaid balance after day 50.

**Q: Is insurance coverage automatic?**
A: Yes. Included automatically at no extra cost.

**Q: Can I increase my credit limit?**
A: Yes. Request after 6 months with good payment history. Annual auto-review.

**Q: Can I transfer balance from another card?**
A: Yes. Balance transfer available with 2-3% fee.

**Q: Are reward points taxable?**
A: Generally non-taxable as marketing incentives. Consult your tax advisor.

**Q: How do I claim airport welcome service?**
A: Contact MGA 24 hours before arrival with flight details. They'll arrange.
