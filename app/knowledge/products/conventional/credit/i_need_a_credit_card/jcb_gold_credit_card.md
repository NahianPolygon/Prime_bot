## FAQs

**Q: What's the minimum payment?**
A: Minimum 5% of outstanding or BDT 5,000, whichever is higher.

**Q: Is annual fee always charged?**
A: No. Make 15+ purchases in a year = automatic waiver.

**Q: How are reward points calculated?**
A: 1 point per BDT 50 on POS and e-commerce. Not on cash advances or fees.

**Q: Can I extend the interest-free period?**
A: No. It's fixed at 50 days. Use 0% EMI for larger purchases (up to 36 months).

**Q: How do I get the lounge fee waiver?**
A: Spend USD 500 during travel, contact 16218 with proof, receive waiver approval.

**Q: Can I increase my credit limit?**
A: Yes. Request after 6 months with good payment history. Annual auto-review available.

**Q: Is insurance automatic?**
A: Yes. Triple Benefit insurance included at no extra cost.

**Q: Can I make multiple EMI purchases?**
A: Yes. Multiple EMIs allowed as long as total is within credit limit.

**Q: What happens after the 50-day grace period?**
A: Interest (1.5% monthly) applies on unpaid balance from day 51.

**Q: Can I transfer balance from another card?**
A: Yes. Balance transfer available at 2-3% fee.
