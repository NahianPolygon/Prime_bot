# EBL Visa Classic Credit Card

Contactless Credit Card

EBL Contactless card not only provides convenience of contactless payment but also offers all existing features of Credit card. It is a faster way to pay with your EBL Credit card for purchases up to BDT 5,000 at participating stores. Instead of inserting your card into the merchant terminal, simply wave or tap your card on the contactless enabled terminal and pay without entering a PIN.
Zero Renewal Fee

EBL Classic Credit Cards come with a unique offer where you do not need to pay the Renewal Fee if you perform 24 transactions in a year. This gives you the opportunity to save by using the card more.
Up to 45 Days Interest Free Period*

EBL Classic Credit Card offer up to 45 days maximum interest free period. Any purchase transaction or cheque is considered for interest free period of maximum 45 days and minimum 15 days.
Great Discounts

EBL has partnered with hundreds of shops, restaurants and travel agencies where you can get hefty discounts on products and services whenever you use the card.
Installment facility

Installment facility gives you the flexibility to buy or use your credit and convert it into easy installment. You can avail any of the following EMI Plan using you EBL Credit Card -

    EBL ZIP: You can make any purchase from more than hundreds of partner merchant outlets and payback the amount over a tenor ranging from 3 months to 36 months without any interest. Please find the ZIP partner list at ebl.com.bd/partner/zip
    EasyCredit: EasyCredit is an EMI plan where you can make EBL to EBL account transfer, EBL to other bank account transfer through EFTN or issue pay order from the unutilized balance of your credit card at a reduced interest rate of your EBL Visa Classic Credit Card.

Free Supplementary Card

The first supplementary card is free per client

Convenient Payment Option

You can pay any amount ranging from minimum amount (EMI amount + 3% or BDT 500/$10; whichever is higher) due to total amount due and you have the following Payment Option -  

    Cash Payment at Branch or EBL CRM/RTDM
    Auto Debit Facility from EBL Account
    Payment through EBL SKYBANKING / EBL Internet Banking
    Payment though BEFTN
    Bill Payment through bKash App
