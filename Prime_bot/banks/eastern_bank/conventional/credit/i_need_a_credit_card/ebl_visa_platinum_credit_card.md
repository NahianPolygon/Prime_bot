# EBL Visa Platinum Credit Card

## Overview
EBL Visa Platinum Credit Card is designed to offer maximum purchasing power with a credit limit of up to BDT 10,00,000. It provides a range of benefits including complimentary lounge access, installment facilities, and a rewards program.

## What is the EBL Visa Platinum Credit Card
The EBL Visa Platinum Credit Card is a premium credit card offering exclusive benefits such as complimentary access to SKYLOUNGE, Priority Pass airport lounges, and a rewards program. It is ideal for travelers, shoppers, and those seeking premium lifestyle benefits.

## Key Features
- Maximum credit limit of BDT 10,00,000
- Zero renewal fee if you transact 24 times in a year
- Complimentary access to SKYLOUNGE at major airports
- Priority Pass airport lounge access
- Meet & Greet service for international flights
- Risk Assurance Program for cardholders
- 45-day interest-free period
- Great discounts at partnered merchants
- EBL SKYCOINS reward program
- Installment facility with EBL ZIP and EasyCredit options
- Free supplementary card for first two cards
- Convenient payment options including cash, auto debit, and digital banking
- 24-hour contact center support

## Lounge Access
- Complimentary access to SKYLOUNGE at Hazrat Shahjalal International Airport, Dhaka, and Shah Amanat International Airport, Chattogram (Domestic Terminal only)
- Up to 6 complimentary accesses per year
- Two children below 12 years of age and supplementary cardholders also enjoy the facility
- Priority Pass airport lounge access with over hundreds of global locations

## Installment Facility
- EBL ZIP: Interest-free installment plans from 3 to 36 months
- EasyCredit: EMI plan with reduced interest rates for EBL to EBL or other bank transfers

## Payment Options
- Cash payment at branch or EBL CRM/RTDM
- Auto debit from EBL account
- Payment through EBL SKYBANKING / Internet Banking
- BEFTN transfers
- Bill payment through bKash App

## Rewards Program
- EBL SKYCOINS: Earn rewards for retail purchases and redeem for travel, hotel, car rental, online shopping, and gift vouchers
