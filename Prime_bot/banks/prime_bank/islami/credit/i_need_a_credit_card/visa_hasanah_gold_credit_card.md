# Visa Hasanah Gold Credit Card

**Tagline:** Power Your Dreams & Enliven Your Lifestyle

Visa Hasanah Gold Credit Card is Prime Bank's Shariah-based, interest (Riba) free Islamic credit card operating under the 'Ujrah' (service fee) concept, offering BDT 700,000 unsecured and BDT 2,500,000 collateralized credit limits with exclusive VIP benefits.

---

## Overview

Visa Hasanah Gold combines premium lifestyle benefits with Islamic banking principles. Operating on the Ujrah concept (permissible service charges instead of interest), it provides comprehensive rewards, travel privileges, and Takaful insurance protection—all aligned with Shariah requirements.

---

## What is Visa Hasanah Gold?

**Visa Hasanah Gold Credit Card** offers:
- **Shariah-Compliant:** Ujrah-based (no Riba/interest)

- **Reward Points:** 1 point per BDT 50 spent

Perfect for Islamic banking customers seeking premium credit solution without compromising faith-based principles.

## FAQs

**Q: What's the difference between unsecured and collateralized limit?**
A: Unsecured up to BDT 7 lakh based on income. Collateralized against deposits/securities up to BDT 25 lakh. Choose based on needs.

**Q: Is Visa Hasanah truly Riba-free?**
A: Yes. Operating on Ujrah (service fee) concept. No interest charges, only permissible service fees. Shariah-approved.

**Q: Can I get both primary and supplementary cards?**
A: Yes. You get primary + 2 free supplementary cards. Additional cards available at fees.

**Q: How long does EMI approval take?**
A: Instant at participating merchants. Complete purchase on same day.

**Q: Can I increase credit limit?**
A: Yes. Request anytime after 6 months of good payment history. Automatic review annually.

**Q: What if I miss a payment?**
A: Late payment charge 2% monthly. Strong recommendation to set standing instruction.

**Q: Is Takaful insurance automatic?**
A: Yes, automatically included. No additional cost or enrollment needed.

**Q: Can I transfer my credit card balance?**
A: Yes, balance transfer facility available with Ujrah charges (not interest).

**Q: What's the minimum payment?**
A: Minimum 5% of outstanding or BDT 5,000, whichever is higher.

**Q: How are reward points calculated?**
A: 1 point per BDT 50 spent on POS/e-commerce. Not on cash advances or fees.
