# Prime Bank Islami (Hasanah) Cardholder Services

This document contains information for existing Prime Bank Hasanah Islamic credit cardholders regarding essential services, activation, account management, and privileges. Use the links and guidelines below to assist existing cardholders effectively.

## Activate Card
To activate your new or renewed Hasanah credit card, please visit:
[Card Activation Portal](https://www.primebank.com.bd/card-activation)

## Setup PIN
You can set up or reset your Hasanah card PIN securely using MyPrime:
[Generate Card PIN with MyPrime](https://www.primebank.com.bd/generate-card-pin-with-myprime)

## Endorse Card
Card endorsement for foreign currency transactions requires verifying your physical passport. Please visit your nearest Prime Bank Islamic or Conventional branch with your valid passport to properly endorse your your Hasanah card.

## Report Damage or Stolen
**Stolen or Lost Cards:** If your card is stolen or lost, this is an **URGENT** matter. Please block your card immediately by calling our 24/7 Contact Center at 16218 (local) or +88022222222 (international) to prevent unauthorized transactions.  
**Damaged Cards:** If your card is damaged, please visit your nearest branch to request a replacement.

## Bill Payment, Limit Tracking & Transaction History
We recommend managing your Hasanah credit card through the **MyPrime** app. 
- **Bill Payment:** Easily clear your outstanding card balances.
- **Limit Tracking:** Check your current available credit limit and utilization at any time.
- **Transaction History:** View your latest statements and recent transactions instantly.
